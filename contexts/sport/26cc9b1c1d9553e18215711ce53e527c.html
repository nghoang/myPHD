Safeguarding in Sport is a partnership between CHILDREN 1ST and sportscotland. We believe that all children and young people have the right to be protected from abuse and harm.


We support sporting organisations to keep children and young people safe. We provide advice, resources, training and consultancy Ð find out more in the About Us section.
