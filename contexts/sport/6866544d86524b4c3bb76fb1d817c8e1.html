Australia’s most respected sports columnist, Patrick writes with authority and wit on a wide range of sports. He has won many major awards for his news breaking, analysis and campaigning journalism.

After a long stint as rugby union editor, Wayne joined The Australian's outstanding team of cricket writers in 2007. He first covered cricket in 1971, and is also an experienced swimming writer.

harrisba@theaustralian.com.au
