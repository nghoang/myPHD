Sport

Children take part in a range of sporting activities. Our emphasis is to promote as much involvement as possible for all children. Whole school sporting carnivals are run in swimming and athletics. Older grades also participate in the Cross Country, District Sports and Winter sports.

Water Safety and Survival Program

A series of 10 x 45minute sessions are organised for children as part of our Physical Education curriculum. This generally takes place over a 3 week period and  is part of our Physical Education program for all year levels. This takes place at the Lakes Aquadome under the direction of the pool’s qualified instructors. Each child is required to participate unless a medical condition prevents this. If this is the case, please make available, to your child’s teacher, a doctor’s certificate.
