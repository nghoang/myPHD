Organisation Aspire Sports Health & Fitness Ltd
 
Salary £12,500 - £13,750
 
Location West Midlands
 
Type of contract Full time
 
Closing date 30/11/2012

Job Description

About Aspire:
Aspire Sports are not your usual sports coaching company. Established in 2005, Aspire Sports stand out from the crowd and are proud to be a Business Associate of the Association of Physical Education.

The company work with schools, the NHS, community groups and local authorities. Our innovative, education-focused sports and physical activity programmes are at the heart of improving lives and are delivered by a team of passionate people who inspire children, young people and families to be 'Active for Life'.

Job specification:
To be considered for the role of Trainee Sports and Physical Activity Educator you will need to have the following:

Personal Attributes:

  • Strive to deliver high levels of performance at all times
  • Enthusiastic, passionate and driven to help the company achieve its vision
  • Excellent communication skills with children and adults
  • Well organised, adaptable and use initiative
     

Knowledge and Experience:

  • Have experience in the sports coaching and/or education sector
  • Have the ability to deliver high quality sport and physical activity sessions
  • Have a knowledge and understanding of effective pupil behaviour management

Qualifications:

  • Achieved a minimum Level 3 Sport/Dance award (BTEC/NVQ/A-Level)
  • Possess a minimum of one Level 2 NGB qualification or working towards
  • Competent in literacy and numeracy to Level 2
  • Paediatric First Aid qualification (or willing to work towards)

General:

  • Driving licence and access to own mode of transport
  • This job is subject to receipt of an enhanced CRB disclosure and references

Working for Aspire:

  • Employed status on a contract of 37 hours per week
  • A starting salary of £12,500 to £13,750 depending on experience and performance
  • 20 days annual leave in addition to bank holidays
  • Mileage expenses, CPD training fund and uniform
  • Opportunity to work abroad as part of the Aspire to Africa charity

Training and personal development:
Personal development is paramount at Aspire Sports:

  • All employees are allocated a fund each year to spend on courses and training
  • Accredited training qualifications in Teaching and Support, PE and School Sport and Healthy Eating and Nutrition
  • Compulsory in-house mentoring programme and industry-related courses
  • Regular performance reviews

Career progression:

Aspire Sports will support you in your personal development by providing you with the underpinning knowledge, skills and experience for the following careers:

  • Progression within Aspire Sports including promotion to Senior Educator, Mentor, Co-ordinator and Management roles
  • Careers within the Sport & Leisure industry, including Sports Development
  • Careers within the Education sector including Behaviour & Learning Mentors, Primary or Secondary School Teaching and Tutoring
How to apply

For further information please visit www.aspire-sports.co.uk or contact James Morgan or James Trowman on 08455082734

To apply complete the recruitment form found on the work for us page on www.aspire-sports.co.uk and return to recruitment@aspire-sports.co.uk by 30th November 2012

Successful applicants will be selected to attend a three part interview
 

Interview dates
W/C: 03/12/2012
