Federer wins first up in London

Updated at 6:06 am today

Roger Federer has made an impressive start in his bid for a 7th ATP Tour Finals title with A crushing 6-3 6-1 win over the Serb Janko Tipsarevic.

Federer's victory in a match lasting just 68 minutes took him to 40 match wins at the prestigious season-ending event, breaking the previous record of 39 held by Ivan Lendl.

Argentina's Del Potro is playing last week's Paris Masters champion David Ferrer in this morning's other group B match.
