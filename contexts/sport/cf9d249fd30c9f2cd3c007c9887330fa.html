Sport

The London 2012 Olympics are in full swing, so we are capitalising on that (although not enough to get us in trouble!). The theme is sport.

Ideally we want action pictures of people playing sport, but if that proves too challenging you can take pictures of sports equipment or memorabilia too. Try for the action photography though – the Photo Challenge is supposed to be a challenge and we like to encourage people to try new things.

Please send your sporting entries in before full time at midday on Tuesday 7th August.
