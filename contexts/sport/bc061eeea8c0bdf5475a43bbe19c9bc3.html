Welcome to the European Network of Academic Sport Services

ENAS Conference 2012 - Lisbon

Description:

"Strengthening the relevance of Students Sport."

Spread the Word! - Using Communication within your Sport Service.

Does Sport make you smarter? - The impact of Sport on Academic Success.

Sport for Health Sake - The Wellbeing Agenda

Community Building - Can Sport bring a Campus together?

The 15th annual ENAS conference will take place between 7th and 10th November, 2012, at the University of Lisbon, Portugal.

The conference will be held in the main building of the University of Lisbon (Rectory). During these four days, in addition to all the exchange of knowledge and ideas, we'll experience the history, culture and traditions with visits to some magical places in Lisbon.

Early booking discount of €100,00 if payment and registration is finalized before 20th September 2012.

We are looking forward to seeing you in Lisbon!

Participants:

Anyone connected to a sport service for a University or College can participate. If your University or College is a Member of ENAS all your participants can subscribe with a discount to the Conference.

New: An ENAS Member is allowed to bring a maximum of 2 students to join the conference (at the same ENAS Membership rate).

Conference rate:

  • Conference fee (Shared/double room): € 846,00 (Members € 595,00)
  • Conference fee (Single room): € 946,00 (Members € 695,00)
  • Book before 20 September for a discount of € 100,00!
