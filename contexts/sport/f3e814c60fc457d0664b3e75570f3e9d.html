Green Moon wins Melbourne Cup - video

Locally-trained English import Green Moon wins a closely run Australian Melbourne Cup, with Hong Kong-based Australian jockey Brett Prebble leaving it to the last 200m to pull out in front. The win in Australia's richest thoroughbred race is the fourth Melbourne Cup victory for owner Lloyd Williams
