After defeating Chi-Omega, VSST went straight to semi-final

Wednesday, November 17th, 2010

The weather was extremely windy, and the field had turned into a complete pile of mud as a result of the non-stop rain yesterday. Although playing under such a terrible condition, VSST, however, would never back down, because behind them, thousands (well, maybe hundreds…) of fans and supporters were cheering and looking forward to our victory!
