Tuesday, 06 November 2012

  • Connect to the world of international sport!

    City events is the annual conference and networking platform that connects ambitious sports cities from around the world with international sports federations and private event owners.

  • Meet your peers from around the world!

    City events is for all city/region/country public officials who are responsible for attracting international and continental sports events to their area. It is for all those who, over two compact days and in one single place, wish to make valuable connections with the owners of these sports events.

  • Be where it all happens: in the Olympic Capital!

    City events ensures city officials to meet important sports. As Lausanne is the home to over 50 international sports organisations, City events guarantees to make the right contacts while attending a high profile conference programme to learn something new & useful.
