After Spain's success at Euro 2012, we debate if they are the greatest national side sport has seen

  • FOX SPORTS
  • July 03, 2012 3:40PM
  • Tweet

Awesome ... Spain were greeted by thousands in central Madrid. Source: Alberto Di Lolli / AP

Spain's win at the 2012 European Championship finals has re-ignited the debate about their spot in the pantheon of sport's great national sides.

Great National Teams

Which team stands as football's greatest national team?

After defending their Euros crown, which made them the first side to win three successive international trophies, you’ve rated them as football’s greatest international side, joining a chorus of football experts to do just that.

Their success – and style – from 2008 through to 2012, covering tournament wins in Austria, South Africa, Poland and Ukraine, has seen the team praised as one of sport’s iconic sides.

Vincente del Bosque's side, and stars like Iker Casillas, Xavi, Andres Iniesta and Cesc Fabregas have become superstars of their sport and are creating a dynasty that will be talked about for decades to come.

But if they are the best football side we've seen, we need to ask: where do they rank in the lineage of sport’s great national sporting teams?

In terms of teams representing their country on the international stage, has there been a better group?
