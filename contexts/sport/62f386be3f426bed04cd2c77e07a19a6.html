Long recalled to Ireland squad

Nov 6th, 2012, 8:14 pm
View comments
Tweet

Shane Long has been restored to the Republic of Ireland squad for next week’s friendly against Greece. Long was left out of Giovanni Trapattoni’s provisional 26-man panel for the game when it was named last week after West Brom informed the FAI that the striker was nursing a hamstring injury. But the 25-year-old produced an impressive 70-minute cameo for the Baggies in last night’s Premier League win over Southampton, which has helped to ease fears over his fitness. Long has now been added to the final 23-man squad for the game at the Aviva Stadium, with Andy Keogh, Conor Clifford, Shane Duffy and Damien Delaney all dropping to the stand-by list. Tickets for the Greece game start at just €10 for children and €20 for adults. Family tickets start at €50. See www.fai.ie for details. Republic of Ireland squad v Greece: 1.
