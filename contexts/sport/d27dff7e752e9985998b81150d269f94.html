Lorenzo clinches title as Stoner takes win

Jorge Lorenzo wins the MotoGP title by finishing second behind Casey Stoner in Australia as Dani Pedrosa crashes out.

Spies ruled out of rest of season

Yamaha rider Ben Spies will miss the rest of the MotoGP season after surgery on a torn ligament in his shoulder.

Stoner takes pole for Australian GP

Home favourite Casey Stoner overcomes an early crash in qualifying to take pole position for Sunday's Australian GP.
