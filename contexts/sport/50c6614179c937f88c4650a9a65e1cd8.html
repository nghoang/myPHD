Annual Sport and Fitness membership - just £120!

With the new academic year arriving, we're offering you the opportunity to buy an annual Sport and Fitness membership for only £120 (valid until 31 August 2013)

The membership includes:

  • access to any NTU sports clubs (membership covers: joining fees*, training sessions, coaching, facilities and subsidisation of trips / off campus activities)
  • unlimited use of all NTU Sport and Fitness gyms and facilities
  • unlimited access to NTU fitness classes with priority booking
  • access to the University of Nottingham's casual swim sessions for only £2 per visit.

Get yours now!

Want to know more about the membership? See our joining options.


* Some clubs may charge additional fees

Olympics success at NTU!

NTU has a rich heritage of sporting excellence. NTU alumni Richard Hounslow took home a Canoe silver medal from London 2012, with Hockey players Crista Cullen and Hannah Macleod winning bronze. We hope to see this sporting success continue through the many current students and alumni aiming to compete at Rio 2016.
