Arteta calls for big Arsenal attitude

PUSHOVERS ... but Mikel Arteta says Arsenal can bounce back from Old Trafford defeat
Published: 05th November 2012
 

MIKEL ARTETA believe Arsenal must show ‘big attitude’ when they take on Schalke this week.

The Gunners are reeling from their 2-1 defeat at Manchester United on Saturday, where Santi Cazorla’s late strike masked a one-sided affair.

Next up for Arsene Wenger’s men are Schalke away in the Champions League, with the Germans sitting a point clear of them in Group B.

Arteta knows after a 2-0 defeat at home to them a fortnight ago, a response is needed.

He said: “We’ve got another massive game again.

“We lost against [Schalke] at home, which was a big disappointment and it’s going to be a really tough game in Germany.

“We have to bounce back. I think we have to show how much we want to win it and go there and show big attitude.

“I have no doubt about the attitude that we have in the dressing room, and the desire.

“I think the attitude was good [against Manchester United]. We all kept trying, even with 10 men.

“We had a couple of chances to score and we have to give some credit to the lads.

“But again, conceding those goals against a big team just kills the games.”
