Why Film Sports?

If you are in a sports team, a coach or just have friends who play sports, would you want to watch the last game? Perhaps it would help improve the game, or answer those awkward questions? This is why we do sports videos. Whatever it is, you want to remember it. That is why we capture the excitement with Sport Videography. We can bring all our teams resources to cover your sports event, or we can offer cost effective budget coverage of your local game. Spend some time looking at our samples and invite us to be part of the next season.
 
Sports Camp Video

We spent a day at a NJ camp filming their Girls Hockey training school to produce a Camp video for the families and friends of all those involved. It was a great sunny day although hot and dry on the field. The whole camp was extremely helpful and cooperative which helped us produce a great video.
