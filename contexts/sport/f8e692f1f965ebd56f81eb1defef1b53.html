Sport Industry CEOs

Hello and welcome to the Sportspeople Sport Industry CEOs Group. This group is open exclusively to Chief Executives, Directors and General Managers to discuss topics in the sport sector and the allied sectors of fitness, leisure and aquatics. As a member of this Group you can read and reply to topics as well as start up new topics. Remember, this Group is open only to approved Chief Executives, Directors and General Managers.

Sportspeople will actively promote career opportunities to the Sport Industry CEOs Group when we are recruiting a relevant role. If you are not necessarily seeking new opportunities you might still want to join as you may know someone else (within your network) that may be looking.

Please note the information posted to the group may vary from time to time and will be dependant upon the availability of jobs. It is not Sportspeople's intention to "flood" Group Members with spam or irrelevant information; so we'll post when there's something to say.

Established 2011.
