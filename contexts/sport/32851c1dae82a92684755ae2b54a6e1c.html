SPORT

with Alan Partridge

Alan made his national sports desk debut with THE DAY TODAY, having previously worked for regional television and radio in Norwich.

A seasoned professional, Alan has a wide knowledge of all sports (as when he went to Marple for the horse racing and asked the jockeys if they were allowed out from school to ride the horses). Most impressive was Alan's World Cup Soccer Meter which was so successful that it was not used again this year.
