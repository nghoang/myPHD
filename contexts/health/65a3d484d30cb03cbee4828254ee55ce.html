Welcome to Momentum Health

The first medical aid in South Africa that gives you access to HealthReturns and lets you save up to 35% on your medical aid contribution.

How can I save up to 35% on my contribution?

By choosing to use certain providers, Momentum Health members can save up to 35% on their contribution, without sacrificing any benefits.

Make the right choice

Momentum Health is one of the largest medical aid providers in SA, covering more than 100 000 families. It offers you:

  • up to 35% saving on contributions through its provider choice
  • up to 4 free GP visits and R5 400 a year in HealthReturns from Momentum
  • complete flexibility and control over your healthcare savings with Momentum's HealthSaver
  • free annual preventative/screening benefits
  • an innovative mobisite for medical information on the go, and
  • one of the most enviable principal member profiles in the open scheme industry

The magazine for Momentum Health members is now available on line.
