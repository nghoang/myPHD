  • We take cancer care personally

    At OHSU Knight Cancer Institute, we personalize cancer care. Our doctors, nurses, pathologists and researchers are dedicated to providing individually tailored and targeted cancer treatments. Discover how we personalize medicine.

  • One of the top cancer centers in the U.S.

    OHSU Knight Cancer Institute is known worldwide for providing the most advanced cancer care from top cancer specialists. As the only center in Oregon designated by the National Cancer Institute, we provide treatment options for all cancer types. Read more about our services.

  • As each person is unique, so is their cancer care

    At OHSU Knight Cancer Institute, we offer a unique personalized approach to cancer care. From our coordinated care teams to our nurse navigators, we’re focused on helping you through every step of your customized cancer treatment plan. Learn more about how we care.

  • Meet Dr. Brian Druker, Lasker and Japan Award Winner, and American Academy of Arts and Sciences member

    "There is no question that we can defeat cancer. What it requires is knowledge. When we understand what is broken, we can fix it."

  • How You Can Help

    Join our fight against cancer by getting involved, providing support and being an advocate of cancer research.

Changing the Face of Cancer

The Knight Cancer Institute is dedicated to providing individually tailored, compassionate care to our patients, and to helping find new, targeted therapies to treat cancer. Our hard work is paying off; it’s truly changing the face of cancer. Meet some of our patients, and learn about the ways we’re working hard every day to end cancer as we know it.
