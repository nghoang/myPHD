The Functions of Public Health to Maintain Health

Health in human life is one component of the welfare society. To increasing the welfare of the community's efforts to improve health behaviors are influenced by environmental factors, health care and genetic factors. Master of public health is science and art of preventing disease, prolonging life, improving physical and mental health, and efficiency through organized community efforts to improve environmental sanitation, control of infection in the community, individual education about personal hygiene, the organization of medical services and care, for diagnosis early, disease prevention and social development, which will support that everyone in society has a strong standard of living to maintain their health without realizing that the area of public health profession is very broad, health improvement (promotion), covering various areas such as carried out in the midst of society, public places, offices, industries and others. Then, disease prevention (preventive), this activity can also be carried out in several places by doing a variety of real activity to prevent the occurrence of various health problems, especially those caused by an unhealthy environment (environmental-based diseases).

To achieve best health for the community, organized effort to approach health care, health improvement (promotion), disease prevention, cure, and health restoration (rehabilitation), held comprehensive, integrated, and sustainable. Public health careers not only work on governments sector, but can work in various relevant fields. The main one associated with the profession, can work as consultant health, hospitals, industrial areas, residential areas, health politicians, and others.


Various problems that arise in the community actually give opportunity and challenge public health professionals. Bachelor of Public Health is a profession that is recognized by the community. Public health courses online have a field study on the epidemiology of health, medical Entomology, Microbiology of health, Health education, and health administration. Technological advances in desperate need of public health professionals who have the specific qualifications.
