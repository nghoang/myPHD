21-Provider Eye Health Services Selects SRS EHR After Disappointing Experience With Click-Heavy Legacy EHR

By MARKETWIREMARKETWIRE12655 Beatrice Street
Los Angeles
CA
90066
USA

MONTVALE, NJ--(Marketwire - Nov 6, 2012) - SRS, the leader in productivity-enhancing EHR technology and services for high-performance physicians, today announced that Eye Health Services has selected the SRS EHR to replace its current EHR for its 21 providers. Eye Health Services has 11 locations in Massachusetts and is one of the premier eye care facilities in the state.

"Our previous EHR was too cumbersome, and made it very difficult for our ophthalmologists to productively document patient exams -- the number of clicks and the time required grew more and more frustrating with each patient encounter. We could not practice like that, so our physicians simply stopped using it," says M. Lisa McHam, M.D., Eye Health Services. "SRS is an EHR that we can actually use throughout our high-volume practice -- its design provides our physicians with flexibility that mimics our individual styles of practicing medicine."

"We want to participate in the meaningful use incentives program, but it was clear that we would never meet the requirements with our former EHR, given the need to capture data with what felt like a thousand extra clicks per exam," says John Mandeville, M.D., Eye Health Services. "The SRS approach to meaningful use is unique -- the data capturing and reporting capabilities are streamlined and fit into our workflows naturally."

"SRS' history of working with large, high-volume ophthalmology groups has given the company a keen understanding of the way our specialty practices medicine, and it is clearly evident in their EHR," says Randy C. Shepard, CEO of Eye Health Services. "Our experience has made us savvy buyers, and we can clearly see the value that SRS delivers to ophthalmologists."

"Ophthalmology differs greatly from other specialties in the way that physicians practice and document their exams, creating unique needs that are not well served by the typical EHR," says Evan Steele, CEO of SRS. "With roots in ophthalmology, SRS has built a solid history of success in the specialty by removing the constraints inherent to other EHRs and building productivity and flexibility into the core of our technology."

About SRS
SRS is the leading provider of productivity-enhancing EHR technology and services for high-performance physicians -- with a successful adoption rate unparalleled in the industry. Offered via the Unified Desktop™, the robust SRS EHR, PM, PACS, and Patient Portal increase speed, boost revenue, free physicians' time, and heighten patient care and satisfaction. For more information on SRS, visit www.srssoft.com, e-mail info@srssoft.com, fax 201.802.1301, or call 800.288.8369.

About Eye Health Services
Founded in 1971 with just five ophthalmologists and three offices, Eye Health Services has flourished into the one of Massachusetts' premier eye care practices. The practice consists of 21 board certified ophthalmologists with subspecialty training in a wide range of areas. Today, EHR now has 11 office locations and is affiliated with two surgical centers. For more information, please visit www.eyehealthservices.com.
