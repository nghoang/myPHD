Notice of Privacy Practices

Location and Parking:

The Pat Walker Health Center is conveniently located on campus at the corner of Maple and Garland. If you choose to drive to the Pat Walker Health Center, please be advised that parking is very limited. If you cannot find parking behind our facility you may park in the Garland Avenue garage (note: there is a fee) or limited metered parking is available along Maple Street.Ê For your convenience, the following Transit routes stop at the health center: Maple Hill Express, Gray, Tan, and Yellow. All other Transit bus lines stop at the Union Transit Center, just 2 blocks south of the Pat Walker Health Center.
