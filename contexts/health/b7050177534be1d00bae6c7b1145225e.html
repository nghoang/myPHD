Prescription Pharmaceutical News from “The Pink Sheet” DAILY

Medicare Payments For Hospital Outpatient Drugs Set At ASP+6% For 2013 / CMS issues its final 2013 hospital outpatient prospective payment system rule for Medicare Part B. The ASP+6% payment rate for drugs is aimed at producing more predictable reimbursement for separately billable drugs, biologics and radiopharmaceuticals. / Health News Daily Nov. 6, 2012

OTCs and Nutritionals from “The Tan Sheet”

Church & Dwight Forecasts Ambitious Gummy Supplement Growth In 2013 / The recently acquired Avid Health business, maker of L’il Critters and Vitafusion gummy supplements, is expected to contribute a third of Church & Dwight’s earnings-per-share growth in fiscal 2013, the consumer packaged goods company says in its Q3 earnings report. / Health News Daily Nov. 6, 2012

Medical Device News from “The Gray Sheet”

BD Gets First Partnered Assay Released For Open-Platform BD Max / BD unveiled BD Max last year as the first fully-automated molecular diagnostics analyzer with an open platform, signaling plans for a smartphone style “app” business model for molecular diagnostics. / Health News Daily Nov. 6, 2012
