Health and Human Services

The Austin/Travis County Health and Human Services Department (HHSD) promotes and protects a healthy community through the use of best practices and community partnerships.

Various public health programs and services are offered: neighborhood-based programs, including Neighborhood and WIC Centers; public health services including maternal and child health, STD and TB clinics; birth and death certificates; epidemiology; consumer health services including restaurant inspections and food handler registration and administrative programs, including grants administration.
