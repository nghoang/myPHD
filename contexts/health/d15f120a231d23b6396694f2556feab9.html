Fall Forum 2012

The NCSL 2012 Fall Forum will take place in Washington, D.C., Dec. 5-7. The meeting of the standing committees will focus on how best to advance the States' Agenda and address challenging policy issues such as human trafficking, budget deficits, health care coverage, education standards, the Farm Bill.
