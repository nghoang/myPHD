Regional Health Authorities

Click on the map for larger image

Regional health authorities (health regions) provide most of the health services in Saskatchewan.

To contact a regional health authority (RHA) or to obtain information on the governance structure, the board of directors and their biographies, click on the specific RHA in the left column below.

To obtain information on the services provided by an individual regional health authority, click on the specific RHA in the right column below. You will automatically be transferred to that health region's website.
