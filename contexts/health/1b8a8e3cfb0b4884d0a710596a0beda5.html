Position: Head of Health & Safety
Salary: £42,000 - £47,000
Location: Woking area

A substantial biological sciences research laboratory in the Woking area requires a talented, senior Health and Safety professional to become their new Head of Health and Safety.

Managing a team of Health and Safety advisors and one Environmental advisor you will play a key role in ensuring that the organisation develops and maintains high levels of Health and Safety. You will maintain compliance with the necessary health and safety legislation, provide competent, timely advice to the Heads of Science and non science groups, support and advise the Director and Senior Management to achieve the effective delivery of Health and Safety Policy and undertake a key role in achieving ISO 9001 compliance with a view to completing OHSAS 18001 compliance of Health and Safety in due course.

Does this sound like the job for you? In order to be considered it is essential that you hold a University degree in Biological sciences or a relevant subject. You will have a proven track record of continued professional development and be a member of IOSH or equivalent. In addition you will ideally boast a NEBOSH general certificate in occupational H&S as well as evidence of work in a biological discipline in a research facility.

In return for your valuable skill-set you will be rewarded with a desirable salary of up to £47,000, a £3000 allowance, pension and many other benefits. To find out more please contact Alex Alderton on 02380 639036.
