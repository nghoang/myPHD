Health News

  • Alert: Laced Marijuana
    Recently, the CSU Police Department and the CSU Health Network have seen some unusual responses in students who have smoked marijuana. Be aware that marijuana can be laced with other illicit and dangerous substances. Click the link to learn more.
  • Flu Season is Coming!
    Flu shots are available at the CSU Health Network. The cost is $20 for students and $25 for non-students. The CSU Health Network Immunizations Clinic is located in Hartshorn and is open Mon, Wed, Thurs, and Fri from 8:00am-4:30pm and Tues from 9:00am-4:30pm. No appointment necessary. Follow the link for more information on flu prevention and treatment.
  • Pertussis Fact Sheet
    Colorado is currently experiencing increased rates of pertussis (whooping cough) consistent with an epidemic. Vaccination is the most effective method of prevention.

Welcome to the CSU Health Network
