Wellness Wheel

Like 'spokes in a wheel' wellness is made up of many components. If one spoke (one aspect of your life) is underdeveloped or neglected, the wheel (your overall wellness) will be off balance. Use the  Wellness Wheel to determine what areas of your life are balanced and what areas may need some improvement.
