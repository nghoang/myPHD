Women’s Reproductive and Sexual Health Forum & Launching of the National HIV/AIDS Policy - Feb 24, 2012.

Many were gathered at the Ministry of Health to participate at the WinLA (Women in Leadership Advocacy) Conference in February 24, 2012. The majority were women of the public service from all walks of life, each with diverse occupations and distinct status within their respective organisations. Also at this conference, the National HIV/AIDs policy was launched.     Read More

Recently Approved Health Policies:

The Ministry of Health is pleased to present the Infection Control Policy and the HIV/AIDS Policy, which was recently endorsed by Cabinet. After much deliberations and consultations with the Health Sector and other stakeholders, these policies have been finalised.
