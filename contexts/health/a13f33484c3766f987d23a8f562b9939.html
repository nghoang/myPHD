OurHealth is a project driven by a committed group of citizen journalists reporting on the health issues in their specific communities, many of which are rural and do not feature on the mainstream news agenda.
OurHealth's citizen journalists aim to highlight the good and bad in clinics and hospitals and also report on those issues which impact of the health and wellbeing of the community.
