Regional Director Nursing and Health Professions - Curriculum, Research and Development

About Corinthian Colleges, Inc.

If you're looking for an exciting opportunity with a robust company committed to changing students' lives and doing the right thing, Corinthian Colleges, Inc. is the place for you. As one of the largest post-secondary education companies in North America, we are on the cutting-edge of the industry and forging ahead into a new era of leadership, growth and innovation. We currently operate more than 100 campuses through Everest College, WyoTech and Heald College and are dedicated to delivering on the promise to our students. At CCi you'll work with impassioned employees and have the support you need to make great things possible.

General Job Description:

You're organized and professional with excellent communication and leadership skills. In this role you will lead designated components of the nursing department in developing strategies for planning, implementing new processes and procedures, and managing ongoing operations. You will be responsible for serving as an integral part of CCi's management team, and for overseeing curriculum and financial planning of selected nursing programs and subordinates. Most importantly, you will use your exemplary leadership and teamwork skills, positive attitude, accountability to company values, and excellent customer service to ensure the nursing program functioning at optimal levels and will make a difference in the lives of many.

Job Responsibilities:

Program Development

  • Develop relationships with key nursing and HP leaders in order to understand related markets.
  • Collaborate with the VP Nursing and Health Professions and CSC leaders to identify key target areas for new nursing programs.
  • Facilitate, mentor and oversee activities and projects of direct reports.
  • Oversee application process and submission to accreditation agencies, ensuring compliance with state, accreditation agencies and Federal regulating agencies rules and regulations.
  • Collaborate with Campus President and leadership in the development of new programs.

Curriculum Development

  • Oversee development, revisions and assure currency of curriculum for nursing and HP programs while adhering to applicable program regulations.
  • Attend national, state and local functions to maintain currency in order to achieve program approvals.
  • Remain abreast of national and state changes and trends in higher education programs.
  • Oversee development of standard policy and procedures for programs, and specific policies as needed for individual sites per regulatory agencies.

Leadership

  • Act as liaison / facilitator for campuses and CSC for operations, curriculum and education.
  • Provide leadership, guidance and direction to subordinate management staff.
  • Ensure training and development needs of team are met and current.
  • Instill a sense of teamwork and connection to CCi's overall objectives by modeling exemplary behavior and coordinating cross-functional workflow.
  • Ensure all requirements are met as mandated by the respective State Board of Nursing, other accreditation agencies and CCI hiring criteria.

Financial Management

  • Manage development of the annual budget for self and direct reports, ensuring alignment with overall CCi business strategies.
  • Exhibit ongoing fiscal responsibility by adhering to budgetary allocations, managing monthly individual expenses, capital expenditures and cost containment.
  • Collaborate with the Campus President, Purchasing, Legal, Real Estate and other key departments regarding program finances to ensure fiduciary compliance.
  • Collaborate with the human resources department to fill open positions in a timely manner, as well as identify improved workflow in order to operate most efficiently.
  • Examine and identify opportunities to improve programs and processes.

Operations

  • Direct, administer and coordinate the activities of assigned projects in support of policies, goals and objectives.
  • Work collaboratively with Accreditation and Licensing to review program applications before submitted to accreditor or state.
  • Direct the preparation of short-term and long-range plans and budgets based on broad organizational goals and growth objectives.
  • Review the results of assigned operations regularly and systematically, and discuss these results with the VP Nursing and Health Professions, Operations.
  • Monitor current trends in nursing education to support success of CCi's nursing program.
  • Monitor and provide guidance to web and marketing development teams to present clear, concise and correct information to others (visitors to nursing microsite, recipients of brochures and other media) about CCi's nursing and health professions programs.
  • Attend and successfully complete all training for this position, as required at any time by the Company.
  • Leadership: Demonstrate exemplary leadership and management skills.
  • Dependability: Respond to requests for service and assistance.
  • Teamwork: Balance team and individual responsibilities.
  • Decision Making: Display willingness to make decisions.
  • Communication: Effectively communicate with others.
  • Time Management: Able to complete all job requirements in allotted time.
  • Work Ethic: Display an organized and results-oriented approach, and motivation to perform without extensive direction.
  • Accuracy: Display high quality of work and level of accuracy.
  • Innovation: Use creative, solution-oriented approach to address problems.

Supervision/Contacts:

Reports to - VP Nursing and Health Professions, Operations

Direct reports- Director, Nursing/HP Program Development, Director HP Program Development

Skills:
  • Doctorate in nursing or related field preferred and Master's Degree in Nursing
  • Current, unencumbered RN license; Nursing Education Certification preferred
  • Minimum 5 years experience in nursing education or comparable education experience, with minimum 3 years in an educational leadership position
  • Minimum 3 years experience of documented Administrative Nursing Management
  • Experience with curriculum development
  • Knowledge of business methodologies and financial management
  • Excellent leadership and communication skills
  • Working knowledge of commonly used computer programs and management systems
  • Demonstrated ability to fulfill Company Key Behaviors
  • Ability to work assigned/flexible hours necessary to complete the job on a weekly basis
