Health System

The Saskatchewan health care system is made up of many provincial, regional and local organizations working together to ensure we have healthy people in healthy communities.

The Minister of Health oversees the strategic direction of the system. Saskatchewan Health oversees and co-ordinates the delivery of health services in the province.

Most services are delivered through the province's regional health authorities (health regions), their affiliated organizations, and the Saskatchewan Cancer Agency.
