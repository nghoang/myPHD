Welcome to Boston and the Crossroads of Technology and Health Care

We are excited to welcome world renowned speakers from across the globe to participate in our 9th Annual Connected Health Conference. Every year, our conference brings leaders in health care delivery & design, academics, and technology to tackle health care’s toughest challenges.

This year, our theme is: Innovations to Build Value, Accountable Care and Patient Engagement.

With over 100 speakers and numerous networking events, attendees will have an opportunity to hear innovative thinking, participate in discussions and network.

This is our chance to come together and define the future of care delivery and ensure better integration of quality care in the day-to-day lives of our patients.

We hope you will join us and the 1,500 other attendees in Boston for this unique event.
