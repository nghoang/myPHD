Job Title:                                  Health & Safety Consultant
Location:                                  South East
Overview of Job:
To provide a consultative service to RPS clients on all aspects of health & safety compliance including fire risk assessments. Identifying issues and making recommendations to the customer to ensure control/compliance of health & safety and fire risk.
Key Responsibilities:
  • Conduct health & safety and fire risk assessments at client sites
  • Offer effective solutions to clients to reduce/eliminate risks in the workplace
  • Make recommendations on compliance/control methods/remedies
  • Compile reports and present findings to clients


Experience/Skills Required:
  • Health and Safety Qualification (degree level or equivalent).
  • Ideally CMIOSH or Corporate CIEH.
  • OSHCR Registration.
  • Previously worked for a Consultancy
  • Excellent communication skills with the ability to interact with clients/colleagues of all levels
  • Able to record data/write accurate reports and present findings and solutions to customers
  • Ability to work to strict deadlines
