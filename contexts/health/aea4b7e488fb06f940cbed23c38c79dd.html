Health Newsletters

  • NIH News in Health — A monthly newsletter containing practical health information based on research conducted either by NIH's own scientists or by our grantees at universities and medical schools around the country.
  • NCI Cancer Bulletin — A weekly newsletter produced by the National Cancer Institute (NCI). NCI, which was established in 1937, leads the national effort to eliminate the suffering and death due to cancer.
  • NCCAM Clinical Digest — A monthly newsletter produced by the National Center for Complementary and Alternative Medicine (NCCAM). Each issue offers evidence-based information on complementary health practices and a specific health condition.

Health Databases

  • Clinical Trials — search for research studies you can participate in as a volunteer
  • Healthfinder — a health resource maintained by the Department of Health and Human Services
  • International Bibliographic Information on Dietary Supplements (IBIDS) Database — article titles focused on dietary supplements
  • MedlinePlus — a health database maintained by NIH's National Library of Medicine
  • NIH Publications List — a searchable database of health related publications from NIH
  • PubMed — comprehensive database of article titles and abstracts
