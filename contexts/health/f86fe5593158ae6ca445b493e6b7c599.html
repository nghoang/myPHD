NSW Health currently employs more than 100,000 people across the public health system.

We aim to have a health system that values its workforce as a vital resource and treats its staff fairly.

We recognise the importance of having a health workforce that is trained, organised and deployed creatively and intelligently to meet the changing needs of health consumers, carers and the wider population of New South Wales.

Whether you are currently an employee in the New South Wales public health system or looking for a job with us, here you can access our job vacancies and information about pay rates and employment conditions and benefits.
