Press Release
Tuesday, November 6, 2012 - 12:30pm

The Country Music Association has launched a new health care program for its members and their families. CMAÕs Instrumental Healthcare program ensures access to affordable coverage with exceptional benefits and more options. The program is a partnership between CMA, Pancoast Benefits, and Vanderbilt University Medical Center.

The Lilly Foundation is donating $200,000 to the Red Cross in support of Hurricane Sandy relief efforts. In addition, the foundation is contributing a $1 match for every dollar donated by Lilly employees to the Red Cross or The Salvation Army.
