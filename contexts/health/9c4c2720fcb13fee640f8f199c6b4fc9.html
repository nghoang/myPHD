Welcome to the Clinton County Health Department

Clinton County is located in the northern part of New York State. We border the State of Vermont to the east and Canada to the north. We offer a wide variety of health services and programs to county residents, geared toward improving their individual health and well being and that of the community. We hope you enjoy exploring our website,  and learning about our local health department.

If you know the specific department you would like to contact, then you can click here. Otherwise you can browse our menu system for the topic you are looking for, see our "CCHD Quick Links" box or use our search engine. If you have a few moments, we would appreciate hearing your comments on our website, so that we can continue to make it a useful tool to the community.


Jerie Reid
Director of the Clinton County Health Department
