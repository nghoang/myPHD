Health Benefits

Health Card and Health Benefits - Common Questions
Eligibility for Health Benefits

Eligibility for health coverage for Saskatchewan residents, students, people moving to and from Saskatchewan (including special classes of newcomers) or temporarily absent from the province.

Apply for a Saskatchewan Health Card

For new residents of Saskatchewan to register themselves and their dependants to be eligible for health benefits.

Update Health Card Information

For residents who have a Saskatchewan Health Services Card, and need to update their registration information.

Out-of-Province/Out-of-Canada Coverage

Some services received outside the province are covered by Saskatchewan Health. In some cases, prior approval is required.

Drug Plan & Extended Benefits
Low Income Support

Benefits provided to low income families, individuals or families receiving Social Assistance, seniors on fixed incomes and those whose drug cost are high in relation to their income.

Supplementary Health Program

Those who qualify for Supplementary Health benefits are eligible for a number of health services and products.

Services Covered by Saskatchewan Health Benefits

Publicly-funded medical and community services available to Saskatchewan residents with a valid Health Services card.
