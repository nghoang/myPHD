Department of Public Health

Evening Hours for Immunizations, Testing:
The Department of Public Health will remain open on Tuesdays until 7 p.m. from Sept. 4-Nov. 27 for immunizations. Testing for HIV/AIDS and syphilis will also be offered during these hours.
 

Healthy People, Healthy Families, Healthy Communities

The Cumberland County Public Health Department provides an array of services to the citizens of the County. Its mission is to prevent illness and injury through clinical services; promote good health practices by educating the public; and keeping the environment clean, healthy and safe.

Federal, State and County tax money fund the Health Department. Grants and fees for services generate additional funds. The Health Department is governed by the Cumberland County Board of Health, which is composed of eleven members appointed by the Board of Commissioners.
