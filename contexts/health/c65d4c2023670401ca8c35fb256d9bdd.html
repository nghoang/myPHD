Health Department

Behavioral Health Strategic Plan

 
This report outlines the recommendations of community stakeholders and service providers on how to strengthen the behavioral health system by addressing challenges and identifying opportunities for improvement in service delivery.

Behavioral Health Resource Guide


This guide provides information on accessing mental health and substance abuse resources in the Greater New Orleans area, including, information on crisis services, hotlines, community and clinic based services, and resources for supportive services. Agencies wishing to update their listing can call the Health Department at (504) 658-2500.

Fit NOLA


This Action Blueprint is a road map for those who live, learn, work and play in New Orleans, to collaborate and take action to improve the nutritional and physical fitness of our community. New Orleans is a Let’s Move! City and is committed to becoming a top ten fittest city in the United States by 2018. A summary of the Blueprint is available in the Fit NOLA Action Agenda. Organizations and people that are interested in becoming involved with the Fit NOLA Partnership can call the Health Department at (504) 658-2500.

Vision

To serve New Orleanians as a 21st century health department and a model for the nation, capable of improving population health through data-driven decision making and policy development.

It is our mission to:

  • Protect, promote and improve the health of all community members so they can achieve their full potential
  • Foster an optimum health-related quality of life for those that live, learn, work, and play in New Orleans
  • Ensure conditions that enable health and healthy choices
