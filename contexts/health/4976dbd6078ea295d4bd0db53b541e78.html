Student Health & Wellness Starts Here



Delivering dynamic, up-to-date content, Discovery Education Health brings classrooms to life with a comprehensive health and prevention library that addresses the needs of the whole student.
 

 
  • Bring subjects to life for students with an unmatched collection of health and prevention resources
  • Facilitate discussions on difficult topics such as tobacco and violence
  • Address multiple learning styles with content that can be delivered in a variety of formats
  • Lesson plans help students effectively understand core health and prevention concepts
  • Access 16 full curriculum programs covering all grade levels and the nine core topics identified by the Centers for Disease Control as being the most critical for today's youth
