Announcements

  • The HeD Implementation Guide is now ready for consensus!!!!! To review the Implementation Guide and cast your vote please see the HeD Consensus Page. Consensus will last from October 29th - November 7th (8 pm EST).

  • Harmonization Sub-workgroups are going strong! Attend meetings or keep abreast of activities on the HeD Harmonization Sub-Workgroups Home or join them by going to the HeD Model Harmonization SWG Signup page. Missed a meeting? Catch up via the Health eDecisions Meeting Artifacts page, where you can download meeting materials and view recordings.
  • Feedback Wanted!: The Health eDecisions Schema is under development and availble for your review and feedback! View the latest version of the schema HERE (in our HeD Google Code Repository). Leave feedback using the HeD Schema Comments and Feedback wiki page.
  • Please review and comment on the Health eDecisions Consensus Statement.
