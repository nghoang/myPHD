Flu clinics offered across the province
Minister of Health and Wellness, Doug Currie, and Minister of Community Services and Seniors, Valerie Docherty observe senior James Montgomery receive his free flu shot from Public Health Nurse, Michelle Lafford.

Get your Flu shot not the Flu. Islanders are encouraged to take part of this year's expanded immunization program. The Province of Prince Edward Island has expanded the immunization program this year to provide all Islanders age 65 and over free flu shot clinics from mid October to mid November.
