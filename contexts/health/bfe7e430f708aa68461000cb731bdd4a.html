About MintPress

Mint Press News (MPN) is an independent online news organization that works to deliver in-depth, thought provoking analysis, reporting and commentary on the most pressing issues facing our nation and world, including social justice matters. Not only do we keep our readers highly informed on a wide variety of topics concerning social, economic, political and cultural issues, from our experts and exclusive interviews, but we also offer our readers comprehensive information, a positive vision for the future, and concrete action steps towards change. In a headline news driven industry, Mint Press News (MPN) provides readers with an innovative form of journalism that includes long form articles and analyses that not only answer the 'who, what, when, where, and why,' but tie in how certain issues relate in terms of democracy, human rights and government -- bringing you more details of an issue to better understand the complex political climate -- nationally and internationally.
