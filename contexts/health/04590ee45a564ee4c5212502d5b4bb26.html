HPV Immunization Announcement

Attention women born in 1991,1992 or 1993, starting April 2012, BC is offering a ONE TIME ONLY opportunity to get vaccinated against Human Papilloma Virus or HPV.

For more information visit  www. immunizebc.ca.

Vaccine is available at Health Services. Book appointment with a nurse.

UVic Student Health 101!

We are proud to present Student Health 101, a monthly e-health magazine that covers a wide range of health and wellness topics for the university student.
