Medic

Band Members:

Ehab Khaled Fawzi: Guitar, Keyboard, Vocal
Ramy Salah: Guitar
Omar Salah: Bass  
Adham El New: Drums

 

 

Biography:

Medic is a progressive Rock Band founded in 2005, mixing originals and covers from all time favorites like Deep Purple and Radiohead. The band has finished its first album “The Dark Side” and it will be recorded soon.
