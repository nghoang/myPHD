1med·ic

noun \?me-dik\

Definition of MEDIC

: any of a genus (Medicago) of leguminous herbs (as alfalfa)

Origin of MEDIC

Middle English medike, from Latin medica, from Greek m?dik?, from feminine of m?dikos of Media, from M?dia Media
First Known Use: 15th century

noun
