Medic

Gameplay: 

Usually played with a large group.

2 teams.

The objective is to eliminate the other team.

The basic dodgeball rules apply.

Each team picks one player to be the “medic” for their team.

Keep it secret from the opponent.

Once a player gets out (by a hit, or from catch) they must immediately sit in that spot where they got out. They are considered a “dead” player. Dead players cannot get any opponent out.

The medic can revive the dead players by touching their arm or back. Dead players may then return back into play.

Typically, a small group of players, including the medic, may all place their hands on the dead player to trick the other team and hide the true medic.

Once the medic gets out, they cannot be revived or try to revive any other dead players on their team.
