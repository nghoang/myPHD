    Update: 4-21-2012

    I thought I'd updated this last year, but I guess not. Within a month of writing this review back in December '09, I was rear-ended hard in traffic. Pretty much destroyed the back of my M3 -- everything but frame damage (thankfully). Thanks to my earlier experience, I knew exactly where to take the car -- Bumper Medic.

    They had the car for a few weeks, but some of that was due to my insurance or getting parts, but also that there was a lot of damage. They also ended up ordering  new equipment that would result in a higher quality (and faster) repair job, which I really appreciated.

    During that time, I came in probably 3-4 times a week to check on the car and check progress (I'm one of those losers that treats their car like a member of the family, so it was like making hospital visits). I was worried (a bit) that my presence would annoy the hell out of the guys there. If it did, they never showed it. They were super friendly, polite, and always took the time to talk to me about what they were working on. I really appreciated it, especially knowing that they were fairly busy at the time with a lot of cars.

    The car came back looking good as new.

    It's been over 2 years since the repair, and the car STILL looks good as new. No creaks, no loose parts, no leaking seals, nothing has lost alignment, paint looks fantastic, etc.

    At the risk of offending Tito, I'll be honest and say I remember first hearing the name "Bumper Medic" and being concerned about bringing a luxury car with significant damage to a place that sounded like they specialized in dings and scrapes and other minor body issues.  For some reason I expected I would need to find a place called "Platinum Collision Services" or "Diamond Body Shop" or something "grand" or "full body repair" sounding if I wanted to get thorough, high quality work done on a new BMW.

    If you're like me with that preconceived notion, all I can say is get it out of your head. These guys are absolutely top grade and know everything they need to know about the body of your car. Having been to their shop several times, I've seen a ton of high-end cars go through there and they've ALL looked phenomenal upon completion.

    You may go in thinking they just do dings and scrapes, but you'll leave realizing that they do every bit the high quality work that you could want or expect -- and they're fair, honest, friendly people to boot.

    If you've got any body issue with your car, don't hesitate to give these guys a call.
