And sam's sister and the Nashville chick are both named Stacie - random or is there some other meaning for it? And finally, they are in Kentucky or Tennessee?
      Anonymous

      The contract marriage thing is common (it’s not nearly as big a deal as you would expect), but very discouraged because legally, it’s fraud. It happens though, because married soldiers get paid more than single soldiers and the difference is enough to make single soldiers take the chance, and it’s not like anyone could ever prove it was a fake marriage unless you were talking about it like it was. If a soldier/NCO meet all the requirements, promotions can happen on the first of every month. I kept Stacie’s canon name because I wanted to, there’s no big correlation.

      Fort Campbell is actually built right on top of the Tennessee/Kentucky boarder. So if it seems like I keep switching between the two states, that’s why. You leave the base through one gate and be in Tennessee, or if choose a different gate you’ll be in Kentucky.
