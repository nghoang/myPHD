        Medic

        Edit
        • History
        Talk0
        2,653pages on
        this wiki
        Medic is a specialization class for Engineers and Sentinels. Medics improve upon the First Aid and Medicine talents and dramatically increase the recharge rate of associated abilities. They have two specializations.

        Neural Shock Specialization: Improves Neural Shock duration by 25% and toxic damage by 40.

        First Aid Specialization: First Aid does 80 extra healing, ignores toxic damage, and allows medi-gel to revive fallen party members.

        Talent Level BonusesEdit

        Bonus: reduces recharge time on medical talents
        Rank Bonus Notes
        7 6% Stacks with Engineer/Sentinel bonuses
        8 9%
        9 12% Neural Shock Specialization.
        10 15%
        11 18%
        12 21% First Aid Specialization.
