General Info

Furniture Medic is one of the largest on-site furniture restoration companies in the United States. Located in San Leandro, Calif., the company provides an array of commercial repair and restoration services, including structural repair, joint stabilization, precision repair of wood surfaces, readjustment of doors/drawers and upholstery repair/replacement. It also provides several furniture enhancement services, such as buffing, polishing, sheen adjustment and color touch-up. Furniture Medic caters commercial properties, such as offices, hotels, restaurants, hospitals and retirement homes.
