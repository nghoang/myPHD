The Loadout

What items to use and why.

Blutsauger, Normal medi-gun, Ubersaw. This is the loadout for a battlemedic that is strongly recommended and used by [<3] Hiccupherphin [AML] -MVN- himself.

The Blutsauger is used for regaining/gaining health from enemies, or to attack pyros as you flee. Use this as you are retreating during a fight and when you are on fire, since it will counter the burning damage.

For your medigun you can use the Normal Medi-Gun - For an escape plan. Another option would be to use the Kritzkrieg to assist your team rather than be individually offensive.

The Ubersaw - Used for getting Uber for the escape plan. This is the favorite item of all battle medics, and the best mele weapon to use.

Rules Of Being A Battle Medic

As a battle medic, you almost ALWAYS should follow these rules.

Rules of being a battlemedic
1: never harm spycrabs
You shouldn't harm spycrabs because they dont hurt you, and you don't gain anything from it.

2: use melee weapons as often as possible
This is your main ource of damage. Use your Ubersaw as frequently as possibleto maximize your damage output on your enemies. It is much faster than your syringe gun and your medi-gun surely isn't going to do damage.

3: never heal unless needed
Your job is to damage enemies and take them out! Not "heal", thats what NORMAL medics are for! You are the guy that goes out there and slices them up with your saw and pumps them full of needles! Not the guy that sits there with a beam of light, cowering behind his team mates.

4: never heal people who bitch about a battlemedic
This is just karma, if someone doubts your skill, or even your choice of class, leave him for dead. They aren't being a team player and sure as heck don't deserve an Uber.

5: never harm people who let you go
People that do this are rare, and you shouldn't waste the time to attack someone who is leaving you alone, just move on to another target.

6: always fight other battlemedics
Take this as a challange, and show them who is the better battle medic. Who knows, maybe they haven't read this article and aren't using an ubersaw, therefore giving you an advantage!

7: never take friendly teleporters
You will miss an opportunity to slice up a noob with your Ubersaw! It may be longer, but you will almost always find a spy creeping around.

8: always thank the target
Make sure you use the "Danka" taunt and if yu are able to afford it, get the "meet the medic" taunt.

*These rules were created and put to use by [<3] Hiccupherphin [AML] -MVN-
