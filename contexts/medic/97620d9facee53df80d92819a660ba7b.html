If you're the sort of person who enjoys a challenge then you may appreciate the benefits of a Defence University Sponsorship. You will not only gain a rewarding career as a Medical Officer in the Navy, Army or Air Force, but also get paid to study at your university, have your remaining HELP fees paid, and enjoy subsidised accommodation.
