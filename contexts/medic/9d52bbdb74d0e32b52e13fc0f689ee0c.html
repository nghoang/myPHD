Product Description

If you are headed out on a trip or just to the store you can have peace of mind that if you need emergency care for any reason that your important medical information is with you at all times.

This unique preprogrammed flash drive allows you to keep all of you emergency information and pertinent health information close at hand. In fact you can hang it from your key chain so that you are never without life-saving information about your health.

The Jakoter Medic Tag is a slimmed down version of our Jakoter Health Tag and can hold up to 128MB of information in preprogrammed templates. No additional software is needed and the templates "grow" as you add more information.

Here is the type of information you can store on your Medic Tag:

  • ICE - In Case of Emergency Contacts
  • Physician Information
  • Insurance information
  • Medication Information
  • Allergy Information
  • Past Surgeries
  • Medications
  • Other Important Information can be added using the Important Information Template
If you love this product and want a binder to store hard copies of all of your information, check out the Jakoter Health Organizer.
