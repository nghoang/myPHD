Epoka Medic Mission is one of the world's leading suppliers of high quality second-user imaging equipment. The savings achieved from the purchase of used medical equipment can be very significant indeed, resulting in many hospitals and authorities being able to purchase much-needed equipment that would otherwise be unaffordable or unobtainable.

We can provide you with highest quality used equipment directly from our inventory and hospitals in Western Europe. Our 3,000m² warehouse allows us to secure a certain level of stock of all types of equipment at all times and you are more than welcome to visit us and inspect any equipment of interest. We sell high-end used equipment equipped with the newest technologies as well as older equipment in excellent condition for lower budgets.

COMPLETE HOSPITAL INSTALLATIONS AND MOBILE UNITS

Epoka Medic Mission specialises in the sales and acquisition of:
CT scanners
MRI scanners
Ultrasound
X-ray
Mammography
Nuclear medicine and PET
CR and laser cameras

We specialise in imaging equipment and we are capable of providing complete hospital installations and mobile units. We can supply for purchase or lease, and all our medical equipment has a complete service history with Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs).

Epoka Medic Mission carries a large inventory of systems, parts and transducers. Call for availability or visit our webpage on which our inventory is listed and updated regularly.

SERVICE, INSTALLATION AND DE-INSTALLATION

Our company has a worldwide staff of more then 50 technicians. We install and de-install equipment in any country and we can provide you with on-site application and technical training.

We can also arrange application training in cooperation with OEMs in Western Europe. Our technical and logistics team has valuable experience in handling projects reliably and safely. We can also arrange service for you after installation. This service is handled either by staff or by our partners depending on the country.

The ergonomic unit features rounded edges that allow it to easily slide under patients for easy bedside or wheelchair exams, and a compact form that fits right into neonatal incubator trays.It is robust, durable and water-resistant, with a detachable sensor cable that makes maintenance and installation easy.

ACQUISITION OR DISPOSAL OF MEDICAL EQUIPMENT

Should you be considering the acquisition or disposal of any type of medical equipment, please do not hesitate to contact us for a friendly and professional discussion.

We maintain a strong focus on quality, reliability and flexibility in our efforts to provide the best possible service to our partners and customers. Therefore, our company has a reputation in the medical world for providing the most reliable, professional and cost-effective service.

Epoka Medic Mission has a large network of contacts throughout the world and we aim at creating long term relationships with our customers build on trust and reliability.
