Mobile technology for frontline health workers and patients.

1 billion people will never see a doctor in their lives, but 95% of the world's population is covered by a mobile signal. Medic Mobile supports frontline health workers, families, and patients in challenging settings. Our mission is to improve health by getting more people into care, improving the quality of care, and helping people stay in life-saving programs.

Medic Mobile provides a vaccination reminder service in India. Photo by Priyanka Pathak.
