South Side Clinic

South Side Clinic Schedule

The South Side Clinic operates each Saturday morning starting at 8:30am and works out of the Access Community Health Center at 2202 S. Park St.

Map and Directions

The South Side MEDiC Clinic was established in November of 1992 under the direction of Dr. Cindy Haq in response to medical student interest in more clinical volunteer opportunities.

 

In early 1996 the clinic moved to its new location at 2202 South Park Street, where it operates out of space generously donated by Access Community Health Centers and South Madison Health and Family Center-Harambee.

 

The clinic has several well-equipped exam rooms, a front desk area and waiting room.

 

The South Side MEDiC Clinic is currently the only clinic that sees walk-in patients only. Most of the patients seen at South Side are adults without medical insurance who come from a wide geographic area. An increasing percentage of the patients seen speak only Spanish, although patients come from a variety of backgrounds.

 

MEDiC operates two speciality clinics at its South Side location. Once each month, UW Dermatology faculty provide specialized dermatology services to both referred and walk-in patients. Likewise, Physical Therapy student volunteers provide PT services at the South Side Clinic on a monthly basis.
