Manager - Technical Training (Furnite Medic)

Overview
Responsible for training franchise owners in all aspects of wood repair and restoration, including the technical process and product application that is unique to the industry. Provide technical support as needed to the franchise network. Conduct ongoing product/ process research and development. Manage vendor relationships as well as assist with product promotion and sales.

Responsibilities
Responsible for training new franchise owners in all aspects of the technical process and product application that is unique to the wood repair and restoration industry. This includes curriculum development (writing/ revising subject matter material) and execution of the training events.
Management of the training facility. This includes buying and ordering supplies and cleaning and organizing after events.
Conduct ongoing product/ process research and development. Stay current with new products and more importantly know how to leverage them within the different service lines of our industry.
Provide the franchise network with on-going technical and product support via: regularly published technical process and bulletins,... View Full Description production of technical training videos and podcasts, responding to email and bulletin board questions, and technical phone line coverage during normal business hours.
Responsible for maintaining the warehouse as it relates to the inventory of our products and on-line ordering site. Includes but not limited to competitive pricing and promotion of the inventory.
Prepare and present live technical demonstrations to prospective franchisees.
Collaborate with and support the management team, operations team, and marketing staff as needed. Includes planning and execution of meetings, special events, convention, etc.
Applicant must possess skills necessary to teach all aspects of wood repair and restoration.
Skills may include precision repair, image enhancement, toning, glazing, stripping, refinishing, structural repair, color theory, etc.
Applicant must be knowledgeable and experienced with the use of tools and equipment common to the wood repair and restoration industry.
Applicant must be familiar with OSHA health and safety regulations as they apply to operating a woodworking and refinishing shop.
Applicant must have knowledge of cabinet construction and possess the technical skills needed to repair, rebuild, and reface cabinets to their original condition.

Education/ Experience
Bachelors Degree and/or 5 years of relevant experience in wood/furniture repair and restoration
Beneficial, but not required to have been a business owner
At least 1- 3 years experience with writing curriculum and managing the training process
Experience in managing research and development processes, preferred.

Note: Please mention GetWoodworkingJobs.com in your cover letter when applying.
