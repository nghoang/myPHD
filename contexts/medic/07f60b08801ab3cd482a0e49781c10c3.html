Advancing and Preserving U.S. Medical Innovation

Join the MedIC Coalition

For decades, innovation has powered the U.S. healthcare system and driven high quality job creation. But now, America’s medical innovation leadership faces threats that could choke off the flow of novel medical technologies and ground-breaking therapies developed here in America.

The Time is Now

With healthcare reform underway and an uncertain regulatory environment at the FDA and CMS, advocates for medical innovation need a collective voice in Washington.

That’s why the National Venture Capital Association (NVCA) has marshalled life science investors, innovators and entrepreneurs to form the Medical Innovation and Competitiveness (MedIC) Coalition.

Our goals:

  1. Bring Medical Innovation to the forefront of healthcare reform
  2. Establish incentives for medical innovators and investors
  3. Achieve broad-based FDA reform
