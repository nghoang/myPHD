Medic Hotel

Only a 15-minute walk from Sofia's central Vitosha shopping street, the Medic Hotel offers you en-suite and air-conditioned rooms, a 24-hour restaurant and a charming garden.

All rooms are equipped with a satellite TV and free Wi-fi is available in the public areas of the Medic Hotel

The winter garden with an open fireplace is great for relaxing during the colder months, while the restaurant offers an inviting garden terrace in summer.

Airport transfers, an ironing service and packed lunches are available on request at the Medic Hotel.
