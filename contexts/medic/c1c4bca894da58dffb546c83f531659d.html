About Us

PowerbookMedic.com started out on eBay in 1998 where we offered a small selection of parts to the growing eBay community. (Our seller id is wallachee) We slowly offered repair services as we saw our customers were uncomfortable doing some repairs themselves. We also added repair manuals so customer could attempt to perform the repairs themselves. As our business gre, we saw the importance of opening a webiste to better serve our customer needs. So, in December 2002 we opened our first online store. Since then we've tried many different styles of websites in order to make the Powerbookmedic.com experience as painless as possible for our customers. We hope our current site will do just that.
