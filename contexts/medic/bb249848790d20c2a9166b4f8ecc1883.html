Medic Solutions, we are providers of event first aid, medical and ambulance cover all over the UK. We are a well established medical services provider with most of our operations taking place in London and the South East Coast.

The company is owned and managed by two Healthcare Professionals with over 20 years experience in the Pre-Hospital Care environment.

As well as offering event services, Medic Solutions are also able to provide on-site First Aid training and Ambulance Transportation services.

Medic Solutions guarantee to offer a tailored service for the individual event and offer advice on how to comply with the current Health and Safety Regulations.
