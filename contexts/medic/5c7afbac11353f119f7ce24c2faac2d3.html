    Medical Education and Demonstration of Individual Competence(MEDIC) Table Training

  • All 68W Soldiers are required to complete Training Tables I-VII and Table VIII Validation annually as outlined in TC 8-800.  Completing Tables I-VII provides the 68W with 48 hours of CEU’s toward their NREMT re-registration.  Completion Table VIII Validation meets the verification of skill competence. 
    Completion of MEDIC Table Training is required annually to meet NREMT re-registration requirements.
    All unit Commanders will be required to MIPR funds for Class VIII expendable supplies for training at the MSTC.   MIPR of funds can be arranged with Karen Steiner at the Resource Management Division at 719-526-0419.  See the MEDIC equipment list.

  • Prerequisites:
     -  Registration forms should be submitted no later than 30 days prior to class start date.
     -  Soldiers must have a MOS of 68W.
     -  Soldiers must have a current NREMT-B certification or higher.
     -  Soldiers must have a current BLS card.
     -  Soldiers must have the ability to lift up to 125 pounds.

  • Class Times and Uniforms:
    - The classes will be conducted from 0900 hrs until 1630 hrs.
    - The classes will not be held on any Post Training Holidays or Federal Holidays.
    - The uniform will be the duty uniform for military members.
