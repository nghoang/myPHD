Review

This is one of the coolest utilities we have seen. It monitors EVERYTHING going on with your internet connection in real time. Not like some programs that update once every second or so, this shows as it happens. Requires a Pentium, and shows you just how good your connection is. The best feature is its real time update of throughput and compression youre getting.
