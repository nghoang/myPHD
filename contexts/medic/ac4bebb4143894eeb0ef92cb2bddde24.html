About This Business

Provided by business

Specialties

The Grout Medic is the leader in the grout and tile restoration market. The products and equipment used are the latest in technology to bring your grout and tile back to life.

The Grout Medic specializes in performing services on your existing grout and tile surfaces. Each Grout Medic is trained to evaluate your needs, prescribe and perform the procedures for you in a professional and courteous manner while exceeding your expectations. The Grout Medic can perform all of our services in both the residential and commercial environment. Our customers range from the individual homeowner to Fortune 500 companies and each individual job is designed to bring your grout and tile back to life and protect it for years to come.

History

Established in 1996

The Grout Medic franchise was founded in 1996 and the West/Southwest territory that includes Naperville, was the first territory.  Since then, the Grout Medic franchise has grown to over 50 locations nationwide, but it all started here.

Since our founding, we've continued to focus on cleaning and repairing tile, grout and caulk...we haven't wavered or chased the hot opportunity.  It's the expertise that we've developed over the past 15+ years that we bring to your home should you choose to use The Grout Medic

Meet the Business Owner: Ron B.

Ron grew up in Chicagoland and makes his home in the western suburbs with his wife and children.  Ron brings over 20 years of business and client service experience to The Grout Medic.  It's that depth of experience that provides the basis for how he manages The Grout Medic.  "Our goal is to provide a quality service at a fair price to our customers.  We will do everything in our power to make sure your job is done right and you are completely satisfied with the end result".  

Ron has a strong team with many years of  experience supporting him as The Grout Medic strives to meet and exceed it's customers expectations.

The Grout Medic, which has been serving the western suburbs of Chicagoland since 1996, provides consumers with a low cost alternative to expensive tile, grout and natural stone replacement by allowing customers to restore and repair existing tile, grout and natural stone to "like new" condition without the high cost and structural work needed with a total replacement.
