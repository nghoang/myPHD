the.Medic Video Series

The National Association of School Nurses and PriCara® teamed up with MacNeil/Lehrer Productions to produce a five-part "news-style" video series about the science and risks of abusing prescription medications.

  • Myth Busting

    Introduces the increasing problem of prescription drug abuse and insights behind teens' common beliefs.

  • Changes for Life

    Teens tell their stories of how abusing prescription drugs impacted their lives, relationships, academics and social lives.

  • What Parents Should Know

    Get informed. Parents can help stop the abuse of prescription drugs by recognizing the signs of abuse, communicating with teens and accessing help.

  • Science of Addiction

    When used improperly, prescription pain medications are addictive. Learn more about addiction and the effects of prescription drug abuse on the developing brain.

  • Media Literacy

    Media literacy experts discuss how to analyze mixed messages about drugs in popular culture.
