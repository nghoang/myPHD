Spyware Medic review

Limited evaluation version of Spyware Medic was tested on clean computer. Full system scan was performed. The application detected 6 items related to spyware. However, all found objects actually were related to fully legitimate SpyHunter antispyware, which was designed to protect the system from malware. It is safe and cannot possess any threat. Screenshot provided below proves that Spyware Medic uses false positives.

Free version is limited to removing only 25 cookies. It refuses to delete other malicious objects it found.

The official site is rosecitysoftware.com.

We DO NOT recommend Spyware Medic.
