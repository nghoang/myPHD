We specialize in furniture and wood repair for antiques, specialty items, millwork, paneling, doors and banisters, cabinetry and more.

Furniture Medic® is one of the world’s largest furniture repair and restoration companies and has established a reputation for excellence unmatched in the industry. Our success is rooted in our dedication to providing customers with unparalleled craftsmanship and quality service. Our expertise is unsurpassed and workmanship is guaranteed.
