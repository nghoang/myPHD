    Called yesterday, drove my car in at 9:30am today and dropped it off. Got a call at 10:30 with details and a quote. Picked it up at 4:30 the very same day. Got three wheels straightened and balanced (a decade of driving takes its toll on aluminum wheels). The shop is clean and bright, and in a good location too. One wheel got a small ding in it, but no big deal.

    My car drives great now. Very smooth. No more vibration on the highway, which I'm sure will extend the life of the suspension and bearings too. Mentioned Yelp and got the 'club' rate, which was a great deal.

    Overall: excellent experience. Quick, no hassle, clear info, etc. You won't regret it.

About This Business

Provided by business

Specialties

Wheel Medic is family owned business specializing in repair and reconditioning of aluminum allow wheels.  If you've ever curbed a wheel, hit a pot hole, or just want to change the color scheme... we are the guys for you.  Chroming, custom powder-coating, after-market wheel sales, mobile repair, touch-up, tire sales, mounting and balancing, reconditioning and straighting... we do it all.
