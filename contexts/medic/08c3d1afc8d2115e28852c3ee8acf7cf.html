MEDIC Support @Cardiff

Support for our undergraduate medical students

In February 2012 we received a report we had commissioned on best practice in the provision of support for medical students nationally and internationally.

In response to this, feedback from our students, and in co operation with student representatives a project was set up to enhance the support available to medical students.

We have now developed 3 mechanisms of support for students:

Academic Mentoring

  • Provides academic support: reflections on performance, set goals for the future
  • Compulsory process; must meet on 1-2-1 basis at least once per year
  • Keep same mentor for duration of studies
  • Records kept on personal file

Personal Mentoring

  • Provides support with personal, social and financial issues
  • Is confidential
  • Is voluntary - access when needed
  • Students choose a Personal Support Mentor from the list provided on these pages

Student Support Unit

  • Provides support for health, complex social issues, exam performance
  • Is confidential
  • Students have a personal Case Manager to support them, throughout medical school if necessary

In these pages you will find information about the 3 mechanisms of student support available to undergraduate students, including how to make a referral.

Contact Us

If you would like further information about the support available please contact us. Our contact details can be found by clicking on the Contacts & People option in the menu bar on the left of the screen.
