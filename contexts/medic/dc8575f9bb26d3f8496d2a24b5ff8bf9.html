NT Medic - Agency nursing | Permanent nursing



NT Medic has been matching healthcare professionals with health facilities throughout Australia since 1989. Today, we place nurses, midwives, dentists and personal carers in roles that help them realise their goals.

Forming a part of Healthcare Australia, the nation’s leading healthcare recruitment solutions provider, NT Medic provides healthcare professionals with the greatest choice of shift and work opportunities throughout the Northern Territory, North Queensland and beyond.

Based in Darwin, our team focus on meeting the needs of our healthcare professionals and clients. So whether you want to work close to home, experience a completely different lifestyle or are looking to discover Australia’s most iconic places while you work, we can help!
