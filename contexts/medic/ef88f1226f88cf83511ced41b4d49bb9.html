Marine Medic

Race Medic

An introductory course for crew and race officials.

Aim: To provide essential life saving skills
Duration: 6 Hours.
Qualification: Race Medic Certificate.

Modules:

  • Initial on-water trauma management
  • Contacting medical aid

Coastal Medic

An intermediate course for coastal boaties within 24 hours of assistance.

Aim: To detail the management of trauma up to 24 hours with little outside assistance and medical equipment
Duration: 16 Hours.
Qualification: Coastal Medic Certificate (Recognised by Maritime New Zealand for commercial qualifications).

Modules:

  • Airway, Breathing and Circulation
  • Injury identification
  • Stabilisation/Treatment
  • Evacuation
  • Documentation


Offshore Medic

An advanced course for offshore skippers and crew.

Aim: To detail the management of medical/trauma emergencies onboard for up to five days without medical assistance
Duration: 16 Hours.
Qualification: Offshore Medic Certificate.
Prerequisites: Coastal Medic Certificate (less than four months old).

Modules:

1. Trauma Management

  • Advanced airway management
  • Oxygen administration
  • Advanced chest injury management
  • Fluid resuscitation

2. Medical Management

  • Medical assessment
  • Symptomatic treatment
  • Administration of medicines
  • Advanced wound closure
