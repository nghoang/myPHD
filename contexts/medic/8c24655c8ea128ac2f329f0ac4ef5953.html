From the burgeoning Phoenix music scene comes a band whose
unmistakable sound and flamboyant style makes them, at
once, supremely original and strikingly familiar. Comprised
of vocalist Chris Donathon, and guitar/keyboard player
Hector Bagnod, The Medic Droid encapsulate the very best
elements of their electronica/new wave forefathers, while
simultaneously carving their very own niche in today’s
overcrowded, underwhelming music scene.
