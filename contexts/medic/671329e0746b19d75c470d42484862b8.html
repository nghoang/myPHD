Medic North Emergency Services Ltd. is Canada’s Premier Emergency Medical Solutions provider. Medic North has been providing quality Emergency Medical Solutions since 1995; including Air Medevac Services and Industrial Ambulance Services.

Our Mission:
• Patient Focused Healthcare
• Client Focused Solutions
• Employee Focused Opportunities

We have extensive remote experience in the pre-hospital industry affording our health care personnel the knowledge and resources required to work independently in isolated settings for extended periods of time while providing unparalleled medical services to industry throughout Canada.

We are currently providing our services to a variety of oil and gas, mining, exploration, construction and municipal organizations throughout Canada, including the air ambulance service for the Government of the Northwest Territories. As part of our comprehensive service we provide various levels of health care personnel and facilities, medical equipment, supplies and emergency vehicles.

Medic North is committed to provide exceptional health care professionals, equipment, supplies and operational support and to achieve the highest level of patient and client satisfaction without compromise. To demonstrate our commitment to quality improvement as well as health and safety, Medic North has added further supporting infrastructure by implementing a total quality management system, and becoming ISO 9001:2008, OHSAS 18001:2007 registered and have recieved our Certificate of Recognition (COR). Our Health and Safety record is exceptional for the past fourteen years. In addition we have a 100% success rate in fulfilling all of our client commitments.

Medic North has a successful history of developing business relations and opportunities with Aboriginal and Inuit organizations throughout Canada and is currently involved with several such arrangements.
