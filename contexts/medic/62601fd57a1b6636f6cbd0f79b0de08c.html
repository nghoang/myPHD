        Medic First Aid®/CPR Train the Trainer

        Overview: The Medic First Aid®/CPR Train the Trainer course follows Medic First Aid® recommendations and guidelines for CPR, first aid, and emergency care for the most common emergencies encountered, in addition to the appropriate techniques for instructing adult students.

        Course Length: 16hrs | Course Required By: Medic First Aid®

        Course Objective: Upon completion of the Medic First Aid®/CPR Train the Trainer course, the student will be familiar with:
        • Instructional techniques to become a Medic First Aid® instructor
        Prerequisites: Medic First Aid® Provider

        Written Exam: Medic First Aid® requires a minimum written exam score of 70% for successful completion and issuing of card.

        Retraining: Every 2 years
