Welcome to a new concept in EMS software development. Medic Trac was founded by a need to provide quality emergency medical software to agencies that otherwise could not afford expensive packages.

The Medic Trac software System is a fully functioning, fully installable complete handheld Ambulance Run Ticket System. It is designed and built by Paramedics and EMT's with over 15 years of field experience. We currently offer a free version of our software and remain committed to an affordable enterprise quality product line for the EMS community. Settings are for Tennessee. These settings can be changed using the free desktop administration program.
