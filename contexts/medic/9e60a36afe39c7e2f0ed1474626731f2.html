Dr. LeWinn’s Medic is a range of skincare products suitable for very dry/sensitive and acne-prone skin.

Dermatologist and allergy tested and developed with the support of the Eczema Association of Australasia, Dr. LeWinn’s Medic is suitable for various skin conditions including chronic dryness, eczema, psoriasis, rosacea and acne.

The range sources high quality, scientifically tested active botanicals that are used at optimum concentration to allow the products to work faster and with higher efficacy.

Dr. LeWinn’s Medic products are free from soap, SLS, SLES, steroids, parabens, paraffin oil, lanolin, artificial colours and fragrances.

Bio-Care Oil

Dr. LeWinn’s Medic Bio-Care Oil is a rapidly absorbing, intense treatment oil with “Bio-Sensi” Complex that is rich in Omega-3 and Omega-6 to provide deep nourishment and help minimise the appearance of stretch marks and scars as well as relieve dry skin. The complex improves oil-spread and allows targeted absorption without greasiness. The formula contains no paraffin oil and features a blend that includes anti-inflammatory echium oil, rejuvenating tea oil and St. John’s Wort. It is suitable for sensitive skin including those prone to eczema, psoriasis and chronic dryness.

To use, massage into affected areas twice daily or as often as required using small, circular motions. It is suitable for all ages and pregnant and breast-feeding women.

Oil Control Day Fluid

Dr. LeWinn’s Medic Oil Control Day Fluid is an easily absorbed, oil-free moisturising fluid that features salicylic acid to minimise congestion caused by excess oil, dead skin cells and acne-causing bacteria. It includes African whitewood, known to absorb excess oil, visibly tighten pores and reduce shine. Other ingredients include Canadian willowherb and phytosphingosine to minimise acne-formation by moisturising and calming the skin.

To use, apply to the face and any other affected areas after cleansing, taking care to avoid the delicate eye area. It can be used in conjunction with Spot-Clear Gel for optimal results.
