    medáic

    2? ?/?m?d?k/ Show Spelled[med-ik] Show IPA
    noun
    any plant belonging to the genus Medicago, of the legume family, having trifoliate leaves and grown as a forage crop.
    Also, medick.
    Compare alfalfa, bur clover.

    Origin:
    1400Ð50; late Middle English medike < Latin m?dica < Greek (p—a) M?dik? literally, Median (grass)
