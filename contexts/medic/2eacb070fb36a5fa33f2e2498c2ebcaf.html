        This article is about a skill for Roland. For other uses, see Lance Combat Medic.

        Medic is Roland's third skill tree, of three. The Medic tree focuses mainly on keeping the party alive, from reducing damage to healing friendlies. Similar to the skill tree Support, only more defensive. His other two skill trees are Infantry and Support.

        SkillsEdit

        Tier 1Edit

        • Fitness: Increases Roland's Maximum Health. (First level: +5% Max Health)
        • Aid Station: Allies near Scorpio turret regenerate health. (First level: +1% Health/ second)

        Tier 2Edit

        • Overload: Increases magazine capacity with all weapon types. (First level: +12% Magazine Size)
        • Cauterize: Shooting team members heals them. This effect also works with grenades and rockets. (First level: 6% Weapon Damage --> Health)

        Tier 3Edit

        • Revive: The Scorpio Turret has a chance to instantly revive nearby crippled friends when deployed. (First level: +14% Revival Chance)
        • Grit: Increases your resistance to Bullet Damage. (First level: +3% Bullet Damage Resistance)

        Tier 4Edit

        • Stat: Killing an enemy increases health regeneration for Roland and nearby allies for a few seconds. (First level: +1% Health Regeneration/ second)
