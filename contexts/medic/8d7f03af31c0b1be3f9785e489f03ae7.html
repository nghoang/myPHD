Registry Medic lets you clean your PC Registry and thus optimize the efficiency of your PC.
The program uses an engine which scans the Registry looking for invalid entries including many that remain after uninstalling programs. Just by pressing a button you can repair or delete Registry entries that refer to files or folders that have been deleted or moved.
Registry Medic even lets you scan and delete invalid data from programs and data from the Start menu in the Registry. Registry Medic also uses a virus definition file to scan for and delete trojans that use the Registry to expand and damage your PC. Each time you use the program it automatically creates a back-up of the Registry which you can restore at any time.
