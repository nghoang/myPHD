    Gloomth Medic Dress with Apron Your Size up to 2XL

    Fromgloomth

     
    Gloomth & the Cult of Melancholy

    "Medic" Cross Dress with Apron
    Sizes Available: XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL, 1XL, 2XL
    Size Chart: http://gloomth.com/images/medic.jpg

    MADE TO ORDER, PLEASE ALLOW TIME FOR PRODUCTION.

    ~~~


    Whether you are tending the wounds of your
    undead minions or just going clubbing with
    an eyepatch on- you're going to love our
    "Medic" dress!

    Available in unbleached raw 100% cotton (as
    shown), black or white cotton. It hangs
    beautifully. Shown with both a large petticoat
    underneath and without.

    The bodice features a wide blood red
    "medical" cross with raw edges. The dress has
    a round neckline and ruffles of eyelet lace and
    red lace at the shoulders. Back zipper closure.

    The dress flares out in a large bell skirt from
    the waist, which can accommodate even your
    fattest petticoat.

    The skirt is trimmed with layers of red lace
    and eyelet cotton lace for a sweet frilly feel.

    This dress is accompanied by a detachable
    white cotton apron. It has an adorable patch
    pocket at the front, ideal for storing your
    bandaes. Finished with a red cross applique
    with raw edges.

    Wear it with tattered white tights for a spooky
    nurse look or with otk socks and bandages for
    a broken dolly style! There is also a second
    version of this dress on it's way!
