No operating system is perfect, Windows is no exception. Although it often goes unnoticed, hard drives are filled with junk. To keep your computer performing at its best, it is necessary to manually delete all of these unnecessary files. There are better ways to spend your time.

Disk Medic can greatly simplify the process of cleaning up your hard drive and save you valuable time. It can detect and delete obsolete files - - recovering wasted hard drive space. It can also erase footprints left behind by IE or various other browsers - - recovering hard drive space and protecting your privacy at the same time. Disk Medic is full of elegant features, allowing you total control over the cleanup process.

What can Disk Medic do?

  • Detect and delete obsolete files
  • Plugins for ACDsee, Acrobat, AOL Instant Messenger, and many more..
  • Detect and delete corrupted shortcuts in the Start Menu
  • Manage/Delete Internet Explorer cache, history, web address history and cookies
  • Manage/Delete the AOL browser cache, history, web address history and cookies
  • Manage/Delete Opera cache, history, web address history and cookies
  • Manage/Delete Netscape 6 cache, history, web address history and cookies
  • Empty the Recycle bin
  • Cleanup the Start Menu and Recent Document list
  • Cleanup the Run history
  • Cleanup the Windows Media Player history
  • Cleanup RealPlay/RealOne history
  • Cleanup your system memory
