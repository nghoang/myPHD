More info About Medic Medic

Bio
Medic Medic is a 3-piece rock band from Pittsburgh, PA. With dual, guy/girl vocal harmonies, a heavy guitar/bass setup and jazz influenced drums, what this band lacks in members, they make up for a with a wall of sound.

Please check out our youtube page to see some live action! www.youtube.com/user/medicmedic87

Nicole Ranalli - vocals, lyrics. Phil Blythe - guitars, vocals. Jesse Levi - drums.

In May 2007, we will be recording our first full-length album with Joel Hamilton at Studio G Brooklyn! In the meantime, come see us live and bring your earplugs!
