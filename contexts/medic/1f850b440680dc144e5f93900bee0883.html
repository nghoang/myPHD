Medic-Vault

from Medic-Vault

The Medic-Vault offers dual-access control using a combination PIN number and proximity card. Companies can install the vaults in a fleet of ambulances and control hundreds of vault doors for up to 10,000 users. The vaults conveniently bolt directly to the ambulance chassis and connect to the existing 12-volt system.

A live stream video camera is also available to install to the roof of the ambulance for additional security and enhanced patient care. The camera offers a visual record of exactly who accessed the safe, recording the actions of those entering.

The state-of-the-art camera also allows for two-way communication with emergency room staff to give the attending physician the ability to view the patient and give instruction directly to paramedics. This two-way communication can enhance patient care in particularly critical situations and possibly save lives.

The 360-degree panoramic view captures the entire ambulance offering greater visibility, security and premier patient care.

The Medic-Vault is available in a variety of colors and three sizes:

  • 8 inches tall x 12 inches wide x 8 inches deep
  • 59 inches tall x 21 inches wide x 15 inches deep
  • 23 inches tall x 14 inches wide x 13 inches deep
