    Raynham, MA

    6/8/2012

    This place seemed great. I completely destroyed the screen on my iPhone 4 & was not looking forward to spending another $600 on a new phone. Then I came across this place. For $90 they replaced my screen. The process was SO easy, I walked in & said "I need a new screen". I handed him my phone & he said come back in 20 minutes. Shocked it was so quick! As someone who uses their phone 40 hours a day for work I was definitely dreading having to give it up to fix. The 20 minute wait was perfect.

    I picked it up & the screen looked beautiful. Brand new & only $90. (I also bought an otter box case after). ÊPaying was easy. I handed him my credit card, he ran it through a piece he had attached to his own iPhone and I signed on the screen. I love technology so this was great. Although I was told the receipt would be emailed to me but it never was.

    I would have given this place 5 stars, BUT 4 weeks later I was putting my case on my phone and when I touched the corner of my screen it cracked. I either have the strongest thumb in the world or the replacement screen is not very strong, and as a 112 lb girl with almost no upper body strength I believe it is not my strength that was the problem. The screens are meant for touching!!!! Bravo for quickness & ease in a tight jam but the quality is apparently no good...... I will not be giving them another $90 to fix my screen again!!!!!

About This Business

Specialties

Specialties
We specialize in iPhone 3G, 3GS and the NEW iPhone 4 repairs. We  service everything from cracked screens, broken LCD displays, missing buttons, dead batteries, camera issues, speaker issues, stuck buttons, WAYTER DAMAGE, etc.

We use ONLY APPLE OEM PARTS! All work is done out of our repair center and repairs are done while you wait. We offer iPhone Insurance on NEW and USED iPhones.

All of our repairs are priced competitively, so if you can find a better rate in Boston or the surrounding ares, we will do our best to match it..
