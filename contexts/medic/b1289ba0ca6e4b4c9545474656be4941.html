About Us

Located in Orange County, CA, Power-Medic is an ISO 9001:2000 compliant, full service power supply repair facility.  We specialize in the repair and refurbishment of switching and linear power supplies. 

With combined experience of over 30 years, our technicians can repair or refurbish almost any power supply up to 10,000 watts.

We offer the lowest flat rate pricing and best warranty coverage in the industry!

Our knowledgeable technical staff will provide you with expert service on a wide variety of general purpose and industry-specific power supplies, including:

Micro, mini and mainframe computer power supplies
Industrial process control power supplies
Telecommunications (voice and data) power supplies
Laboratory power supplies
Three phase power supplies
Simulator power supplies
Audio / video power supplies
Transmitter power supplies
Automated test equipment (ATE) power supplies
Printer / FAX  power supplies
Custom power supplies
Medical equipment power supplies
DC / DC converter power supplies
Battery chargers
