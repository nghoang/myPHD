Day One: Introducing the Battle Medic

This post is part of a series "20 Days of... WoW Blogging Challenge", a blogging challenge suggested by Saga at Spellbound. She proposes twenty questions to be answered in twenty days and I, foolishly, have decided to take up this most daunting task because clearly my schedule is not full enough.


DAY ONE: INTRODUCING THE BATTLE MEDIC

My name is Fannon, a Dwarven battle medic hailing from the great realm of Sargeras.  I’m a raid healer and a new dad trying to find a balance between the two.  So far, the baby is winning.

That much you already know, assuming you’re aware of which blog you're on and didn't navigate here by mistake.

This blogging challenge that Saga has put out there is interesting in that the twenty questions are more personal and less about the game that this blog covers, so hopefully by the end of this series of posts you’ll know a great deal more about me than you do now.  Whether or not that is of any interest to you is another matter entirely.

I am really looking forward to participating in this blogging challenge.  Aside from participating in the occasional Blog Azeroth Shared Topics, I have never been involved in any blogging community activities and I’m excited to be doing this one.

I plan to do one post a day throughout April. This is going to be a serious challenge for me as I have never had to keep to a posting schedule before, and I'm hoping that I can provide an interesting challenge post for you everyday and still write the other articles that I have planned. I hope you enjoy the ride (and likely inevitable failure).

Tomorrow,  DAY TWO:  WHY I DECIDED TO START BATTLE MEDIC (or The Clinical Treatment of Obsessive Blog Envy).
