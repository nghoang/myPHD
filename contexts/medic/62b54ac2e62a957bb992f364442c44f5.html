    Welcome to Watch Medic

    Watch Repair is our core business, no matter which Brand you need to Repair we have  skilled Watchmaker at Watch Medic. Indeed any watch that needs attention, Watch Medic is one of the most respected watch repair companies. In Watch Repair we have years of experience throughout all areas of the watch industry. We understand how the costs of watch servicing & repairs through some watch houses and high street jewellers can be unreasonably expensive. Accordingly, we aim to provide a fast, efficient and affordable service for all your horological requirements,We are located in 5 Points Across from Andy's Deli.

    		 
    Company Profiles

     

    Watch Medic  is an established trade watch repair and restoration business.

    We offer a full wristwatch service and repair facility direct to the public. We provide a service on most Swiss brands, specializing in Rolex and Breitling we will also look at some Omega, Patek Phillipe, and Audemars Piguet (the depending upon availability of parts).

    Our Watchmaker apprenticed at Yesterday Jeweler under Bill Messier Master Watch Maker, Graduated as a CW21 Watchmaker,(SWTC) Swiss Watch Technical Center, Chronometer Club,NAWCC and we keep updating our skills
