Program Officer

Transatlantic Leadership Initiatives, German Marshall Fund of the United States

Nonprofit; 51-200 employees; International Affairs industry

Fellowship programs include the Marshall Memorial Fellowship (U.S. and Europe), Manfred Worner Seminar (U.S. and Germany), Asmus Policy Entrepreneurs Fellowship (U.S. and Europe) and APSA Congressional Fellowship (Germany).

Program Officer

Nonprofit; 51-200 employees; International Affairs industry

May 2011 – November 2011 (7 months) Belgrade, Serbia

Distributed small to medium size grants in Croatia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, and Serbia to local initiatives aimed at linking citizens with government and improving regional cooperation. The total value of the grantmaking portfolio was $360 thousand.

Chief Whip of the Democratic Party at the Municipal Assembly

Municipality of Savski Venac

Government Administration industry

May 2008 – November 2011 (3 years 7 months) Belgrade

Coordinated sixteen deputies at the municipal assembly and chaired the assembly's committee on budget, finance, and the economy. Other responsibilities included coordination with the chief whips of other governing coalition parties, communication with the chief whips of opposition parties, and reporting of decisions of the committee on budget to the assembly.

Program Associate

Fellowship Programs, Balkan Trust for Democracy, German Marshall Fund of the United States

Nonprofit; 51-200 employees; International Affairs industry

May 2009 – May 2011 (2 years 1 month) Belgrade, Serbia

Coordinated the implementation of Travel to Europe and Marshall Memorial Fellowship with partner organizations in the Balkans. The former consisted of participant selection and facilitation of one month of travel for high achieving university students to EU member states, while the latter of the selection and hosting of midcareer political, business, media, and civil society leaders in an exchange program between Europe and United States. The total value of Travel to Europe was €150 thousand anually, and of the Balkan component of the Marshall Memorial Fellowship, €60 thousand anually.

Program Assistant

Bulgaria Fund, Balkan Trust for Democracy, German Marshall Fund of the United States

Nonprofit; 51-200 employees; International Affairs industry

September 2007 – May 2009 (1 year 9 months) Belgrade, Serbia

Took part in the start up and implementation of the Bulgaria Fund headed by Program Officer Pavlina Filipova. The project distributed small to medium size grants to local initiatives to advance the rule of law, increase economic competitivness, and integrate vulnerable groups. The total value of the project was $3 million.
