    Vancouver, BC

    10/22/2009

    I rate this computer store: Ê 10001110101010100011111010101

    Now if you are a nerd like I am, you'd think that joke was funny. If not, apologies but I'm not taking it back.lol. PC Medic is a great computer store in Halifax. Reviewer Ang M. took the words out of my mouth when they said it is second-best to the other computer store in the city Greenlyph. I agree.

    However, what Greenlyph doesn't have is a nice display area of computers, computer parts and computer accessories. At PC Medic, you can walk around and shop while at Greenlyph, you walk in, and for the most part you go right up to the counter.

    PC Medic also has great deals on bulk DVDs and other items from time to time. I have rarely found better deals with their DVDs for example. So for regular computers and computer accessories, I always tend to price check between PC Medic and Greenlyph and then leave all the other places alone. These are the only two stores that have real consistent deals and great overall pricing.

    Their computer repair department is also really great! I have had a laptop or two in there in the past, before I bought my Macbook Pro. Now, I tend not to see them for my repair needs but PC Medic is fully equipped to help out. They even make House calls.

    The staff are very knowledgeable about all types of computers and do the best they can to make sure your needs are met. ÊThese aren't your typical, "I'm doing this for the money," staff. They know and love computers and it shows.

    If you want some computer accessories, computers or need a computer repaired, call up PC Medic. They are one of the strongest competitors and for good reason.
