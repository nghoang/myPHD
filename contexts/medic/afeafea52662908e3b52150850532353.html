Preserving Program Integrity

Medicare Advantage (Part C) and Part D programs lose millions of dollars to fraud, waste, and abuse each year.

This website was created to help those committed to stopping Medicare Advantage and Part D fraud, waste and abuse, by providing:

  • relevant general news and information
  • industry-specific tools and resources

By working together, stakeholders and CMS can reduce fraud, waste and abuse and safeguard the integrity of the Medicare Advantage and Part D programs.
