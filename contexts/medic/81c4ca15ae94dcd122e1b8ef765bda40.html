Aerodynamic drag figures don't mean much if your car isn't moving. Even high technology needs down to earth attention.
  Late model vehicles with overdrive and lock-up functions rely on a properly tuned engine and transmission to perform the way they were engineered.
  Bad seals or low fluid can stop any advance in design, so your
automatic transmission should be serviced at regular intervals to maintain maximum efficiency. A simple check-up can prevent major damage and expense from occurring later on.
Leaking fluid is often a warning sign.

Keeping the fluid clean and cool can prolong the life of your transmission.
