The Medic Droid Reunites!!

iarchibald March 12, 2012 0

The Medic Droid was an electro-pop/electro-rock band that came out of Phoenix, Arizona in 2005. Consisting of members Chris Donathon, Hector Bagnod, and Johnny “Droid” Chavez, the group accumulated most of its fan base through the social-networking site MySpace, with many of their songs receiving over a million plays each.

Heavily inspired by 1980’s pop culture–the band’s name stems from a character in the Star Wars sequel “The Empire Strikes Back”–The Medic Droid received noticeable success for their debut album What’s Your Medium? in 2008. What’s Your Medium? was recorded after being signed by Modern Art Records, an offshoot of the Epic Records label. The album showcased The Medic Droid’s notable fun party style with singles like “Fer Sure”, “Fscene8”, and “The Killer Anna”, as well as a cover of Madonna’s anthem “Into The Groove”.

The Medic Droid began co-headlining their first of three tours in 2008 alongside similar-sounding bands including A Cursive Memory, Breathe Carolina, The White Tie Affair, Kill Hannah, Innerpartysystem, The Blackout, From First To Last, and We Are The Ocean.

The Medic Droid set out on one final tour as the sole headlining act in November 2008 with supporting acts Hyper Crush and Chronic Future. The tour was not initially intended to be the band’s last, but the strain of constant touring had worn on the members. In December 2008, both Donathon and Bagnod posted on their individual MySpace pages that The Medic Droid had split, leaving many fans upset in the announcement’s wake.

In the following years, lead singer Donathon announced that he would be beginning a solo project for fans, but it failed to get off the ground. Johnny Droid went on to create his own solo band, “J0hnny”, and later another band with S3RL and Greg Rudawski called “R0bots”.
The band’s former touring buddies Innerpartysystem, Breathe Carolina, and Hyper Crush all proceeded to hit mainstream success, leaving The Medic Droid behind as a memory.

That is, until Donathon announced the band’s reunion last month on February 08th, 2012.

“I’m currently working on putting together my new live set as well as some new tracks,” Donathon said. “I’m super excited to get back on the road again and play some shows, but I need all you guys and girls’ help to spread the word.”

Online website AbsolutePunk confirmed The Medic Droid’s reunion on February 19th, 2012.

The response has been positive and enthusiastic following the announcement, with many fans flocking to the band’s newly founded Facebook fan page to post their reactions.

“I screamed and ran around my house with joy!” admits Riah Louise, a long-time fan of The Medic Droid. “I am so glad that The Medic Droid is coming back!”

“Excuse me,” said Rhiannon Morgan upon hearing the news, “I need a moment to sob hysterically out of pure happiness.”

“I am so incredibly happy to hear that The Medic Droid is back,” says Rudi Davidson. “They are seriously the best band ever. ‘The Killer Anna’ is the one song I know that I can listen to over and over again and never turn it off. They have all of my support!”

Right now, the Facebook fan page and all band updates are being maintained solely by Donathon, who created the page. An official line-up has yet to be announced, as well as the status of if Bagnod and Droid will make their reappearance alongside Donathon.

“The line-up is still being lined up,” quips Donathon, “but I’m way excited about it and will have more news on that very soon.”

To show your support to The Medic Droid and receive up-to-the-minute updates, be sure to like their official Facebook fan page: “The Medic Droid (OFFICIAL)”.

Article written by Angela Nicole Holbrook.
