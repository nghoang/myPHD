About This Business

Provided by business

Specialties

Regrouting ~ .Our process of grout removal and replacement will give you that "new" look at a fraction of the cost of replacement. The job is performed in one day without the hassle of removal and installation.

Clearning & Sealing ~ We apply a solution to your floor that is designed to pull dirt out of the pores of grout joints. Our advanced steam vapor cleaning process eliminates traffic patters in your grout. A clear, penetrating sealer is applied to protect your grout joints from future discoloration.

Staining ~ An attractive option for customers who want a different look, either to brighten a room or simply change the appearance.

Tile Replacement ~ We rescue loose tiles without removing them from the floor and perform the necessary grout repairs to make your floor look like new again.

Caulking ~ New caulking around the tub and or shower edges provides that clean look you have been trying to achieve.

History

Established in 2006

When it comes to restoring grout, we are proud to serve as the industry experts. Grout Medic technicians specialize in tile and grout and our technologies, coupled with our unparalleled customer service, have made us the most respected brand in the industry. Rest assured that each technician that visits your home has completed a rigorous training program and has earned a certification that reflects our strict standards of care. Your flooring is in "good hands" with The Grout Medic and the results will be amazing.
Superior service is a hallmark of the Grout Medic brand. Our technicians work hard to provide courteous, professional and reliable customer service, making the experience as enjoyable as the results. A written estimate is provided for each job, and our technicians are fully insured. When you engage a Grout Medic technician, you can be assured that you will receive only the very best in service and results.
