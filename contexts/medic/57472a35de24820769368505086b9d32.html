[edit] Description

The Medic is a Terran ground support unit. It heals your biological units.

Terran Medics can heal biological units. Moreover, their Heal ability can be set to autocast. With autocast active, an idle or patrolling Medic will automatically heal any damaged biological unit within a close proximity.

[edit] Offensive Usage

Medics cannot attack, but they accompany offensive biological units for support.

[edit] Defensive Capabilities

Medics are generally used simply for healing your biological units.

[edit] Campaign Specifics

Available in mission: The Outlaws
Mercenary version: None
Hero version: Egon Stetmann
