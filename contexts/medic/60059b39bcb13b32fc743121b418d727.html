        Medic
        A typical Medic
        Tier:4
        Root: Biotechnician
        Branches: none


        OverviewEdit

        The Medic is a tier 4 character class, specialized in supporting other units.

        Description from the official website:

        "Medics start as Specialists and never stray from the role of group support. These tireless officers are constantly at the ready for healing and adding to their squad’s power supply.
        The larger a squad, the more powerful a Medic becomes. These are perhaps the least viable troops for solo deployment."

        Abilities and trainingsEdit

        In addition to the abilities and trainings (collectively known as skills) of all its root classes, the following are available to the Medic class:

        Abilities Trainings
        • Regeneration Wave (Signature ability)
        • Disease
        • Mind Control
        • Viral Conversion
        • Resistance
        • Insanity (Replaced by Mind Control in 1.3.2.1)
        • Leech (Replaced by Viral Conversion in 1.3.2.1)
        • Resuscitate (Replaced by Disease in 1.3.2.1)
        • None
