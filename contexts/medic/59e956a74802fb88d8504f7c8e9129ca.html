Description

With foot maintenance and care at the forefront of its design, the improved Adventure Medical Blister Medic rounds out the blister family of products by bringing together GlacierGel, Moleskin, antiseptic towelettes, and alcohol wipes into one complete ultralight foot care kit.

Specifications

  • Includes: GlacierGel, Moleskin, antiseptic towelettes, and alcohol wipes into one complete ultralight foot care kit
  • Dimension: 4.25 x 6.5 x .5
  • Weight: 1.9 oz.
