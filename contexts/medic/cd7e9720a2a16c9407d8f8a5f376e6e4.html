Furniture Medic

We specialize in on-site restoration of all types of wood furnishing and fixtures including antiques, specialty items, millwork, paneling, doors and banisters, cabinetry and more.

Furniture Medic is one of the largest furniture repair and restoration companies and has established a reputation for excellence unmatched in the industry. Our success is rooted in our dedication to providing customers with unparalleled craftsmanship and quality service. Our expertise is unsurpassed.

Our services include:

Precision repair of nicks, scratches and gouges
Removal of heat and water marks
Restoration refinishing
Stripping and refinishing
French polishing
Insurance inspection and correction
Upholstery repair
Wood finish deluxing
Laminate and hardwood floor repair
Leather repair
Motion furniture repair
Furniture assembly
Re-gluing and structural repair
Fire and water damage repair
Caning and millwork
Moving damage repair
Antique restoration
Pet damage restoration
Kitchen cabinet restoration
