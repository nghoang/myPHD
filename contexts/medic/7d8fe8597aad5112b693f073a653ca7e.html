Diver Medic Technician (DMT) Course

DDRC IMCA Diver Medic Technician (DMT) Course
DDRC IMCA Diver Medic Technician (DMT) Refresher Course

The Diver Medic Technician (DMT) is a person who will be expected to perform a range of advanced medical techniques in an emergency. The DMT might be expected to assist in the initial diagnosis, tend the patient, administer medication and/or fluids and take control of the situation until a Diving Doctor arrives. Effective incident management and communication skills are emphasised during the course.

  • Airway management
  • Shock management
  • Respiratory and Cardiac arrest
  • Care of casualties on site and during Transportation
  • Wound management
  • Diving Accidents
  • Use of Medical
  • Equipment taken to Remote Sites
  • Care and Treatment of the patient in the
  • Hyperbaric Environment
  • Oxygen Administration
  • Medical Record keeping
  • Intravenous Infusion
  • Liaison with Medical Services

Prerequisites/Entry Requirements

  • No prior knowledge or experience is needed, a reasonable standard of First Aid is required to pass the course and a basic knowledge of diving and physics and human biology would be helpful.

Course Duration

  • 10 days, Mon - Fri, 08.30 - 16.30 over a consecutive period of 2 weeks

Certification

  • IMCA Ð Certified Diver Medic Technician Ð Valid for 3 Years
