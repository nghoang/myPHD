About Us

Vision

Vision of our company is to disseminate knowledge and expertise on positron emission tomography (PET) and other tomographic methodologies in Molecular Imaging and make them available worldwide.

 

Mission

RM Medic-Tech offers a wide array of services, including consulting, product solutions, education and training within the development and deployment of medical technologies, specifically targeted towards Positron Emission Tomography (PET) both preclinical and clinical However, such services are also available for Computed Tomography (CT), Single Photon Emission Tomography (SPECT), and Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI). Furthermore, RM Medic-Tech AB provides staff hiring services, including recruitment and staffing in medical technology, education and research.
