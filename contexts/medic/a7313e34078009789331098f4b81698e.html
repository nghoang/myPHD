Stichting Medic Foundation
    Medical Equipment Distribution Information Centre
    What is Medic?
    Medic is an organisation helping to provide medical goods and services to people in need in economically underdeveloped areas, irrespective of race, religion or creed

     

     
    What does Medic do?
    Medic collects and repairs materials useful to health projects sponsored by relief organisations or private persons. Approximately 200 projects are performed annually. Based on requests from the field Medic experts design and manufacture basic medical products especially for use in rural areas, keeping in mind that power-failures occur, spare parts are often hard to get and service may be a problem.
    Examples of our own products are: operating table, operating lamp, delivery bed. Careful consideration is given to the appropriate usage of the goods.

    To achieve this contacts are maintained with Dutch and native workers in many places in Africa, Eastern Europe, Asia, and Central- and South America.

    Medical specialists and technicians are made available by Medic to work locally as Consultants and to facilitate the transfer of expertise.
