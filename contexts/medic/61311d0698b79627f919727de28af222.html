About This Business

Specialties

Phone Medic Hawaii provides quality cellular phone, iPhone, and iPad repair services at a fraction of the cost of buying a new phone.  We take pride in every repair and will treat your phone as if it were our own.  Estimates are free and if your phone is unrepairable, there is no charge.  All repairs are backed by a 90-day warranty.

Repair Services:  
Flex Cable, Water Damage, Broken Screen (LCD), Microphone, Speaker, Volume Switch, Ringtone Buzzer, Charging Connector, Keypad Repair, Sim Card Reader, GSM Phone Unlocking, OEM Housing Refurbishing

For more information please call (808) 589-8899.

History

Established in 2011

Everyone always said that I was good at fixing things and I always enjoyed taking things apart to see what was inside.  I have been fixing electronics for friends for years and decided to take a Master Technician course to be Certified as a Level III Cellular Phone Repair Technician.

Phone Medic Hawaii was established to give customers an option to buying new phones when they don't really need to.  If the repair is not covered by the warranty, out of the warranty period, and you are not within your upgrade window; give us a call.

I understand that everyone is busy and now days not having your cellular phone is like not having your wallet.  We will strive to provide quality service while having your phone back in your hands as quickly as possible.  Customer service will be a priority.

Meet the Business Owner: Kenny A.

Grew up in Hawaii, served my country on active duty for 13 years, and moved home to be close to family.  I am grateful for all that I have and always try to treat everyone with the dignity and respect they deserve.  I have a 4 year old son that I adore and enjoy spending time with.  This is my first business venture and I look forward to the challenge of owning my own business.  Come by and check us out!
