Description

MOBILE MEDIC

Mobile Medic allows you to use a range of simulated diagnostic techniques to identify and treat portrayals of medical emergencies using your iPhone or iPad.

This diagnostic simulator has been adapted in consultation with Medical Officers in the Australian Defence Force.

To test your skills simply download the FREE Mobile Medic app, and then hold your mobile device up to one of the patients, either online, printed out, or on one of the posters. Good luck.

To view patients visit defencejobs.gov.au/mobilemedic
