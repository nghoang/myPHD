Furniture Medic

Date of Incorporation: 1992

Franchising Since: 1992

Address: Memphis, Tennessee

Country:U.S.

Description: The franchisor operates a furniture, fixtures, wood restoration, repair, re-facing and refinishing business. These services are marketed to hotels, homeowners, moving companies, antique dealers, furniture rental companies, insurance companies and others whose responsibilities include residential and commercial property management and maintenance.

Franchise Offer: The franchisor offers a furniture restoration, re-facing, repair and refinishing franchise service business. Each franchised business includes the right to the use the name, service marks and business methods in a non ¬exclusive Territory. The franchisee is encouraged to use their home as a base of operation for the Franchise business until a level of business will support an outside office and warehouse space. As the owner of the Franchise business, they will operate one (or more) service vehicles, in addition to a fixed location furniture restoration service, that displays Furniture Medic's logo and phone number.

Financial Assistance: The franchisor does not offer direct financing. Its affiliate, SMAC, does offer financing for the initial franchise fee and the Opening Equipment Package, including shipping, handling and sales tax charges that you pay to Furniture Medic for your franchise. Furniture Medic does not receive payment for the placement of your financing with SMAC nor does Furniture Medic arrange financing from sources other than SMAC. The franchisee may choose another lender or finance company.

Training and Assistance: Training is conducted in a classroom environment, in a "hands-on" laboratory environment in Furniture Medic’s Training Center in Memphis, Tennessee. Each technical subject includes hands-on guided application and practice time. Training, marketing, and operation manuals are provided as instructional material.

Territory: The franchisee will not receive an exclusive territory. The non-exclusive area corresponds to one or more counties, parishes or other similar designations and must contain at least 100,000 people unless mutually agreed upon between the parties. The franchisee may face competition from other franchisees, from company-owned businesses, or from other channels of distribution or competitive brands.

Term of Agreement and Renewal: Initial franchise term is five years, with an option to renew for an additional five-year term if the franchisee is in full compliance with the franchise agreement.

Obligations and Restrictions: Furniture Medic does not require that the franchisee personally supervise the franchise business. If the franchisee does not personally supervise the business, they must employ a manager who will be responsible for direct, on-premises supervision of the business. The manager must have successfully completed the Furniture Medic training program.

Total Number of Units: Approximately 500 locations.
