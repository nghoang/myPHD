Hoof Medic

This treatment applies to the following species:
  • Horses
Manufacturer: AHC

Concentrated Nutritional Feed Supplement With Biotin, Methionine, Lysine, Omega 3 And Omega 6 Fatty Acids And More

Up To A 60-day Supply

EACH OUNCE CONTAINS (not less than): Biotin 20mg, l-Lysine 2150mg, dl-Methionine 3000mg, Ascorbic Acid 1290mg, Zinc 250mg

Ingredients

Full Fat Active Soy, Yeast Culture (a source of alanine, arginine, aspartic acid, cystine, glycine, glutamic acid, histidine, isoleucine, leucine, lysine, methionine, phennylalanine, proline, serine, threonine, tryptophan, tyrosine, valine, Vitamin A, Vitamin B1, Vitamin B2, Vitamin B6, Vitamin B12, Vitamin E, choline, iron, niacin, inositol, pantothenic acid, calcium, copper, iodine, magnesium, manganese, phosphorus, potassium, selenium, zinc), Bee Pollen, l-Lysine, D-Alpha Tocopheryl, Folic Acid, Methylsulfonylmethane, dl-Methionine, Ascorbic Acid, Zinc Sulfate, Melojel, Calcium Carbonate, Silica, Natural Plant Fiber, Mineral Oil

Hoof Medic Indications

AniMed’s Hoof Medic contains all of the essential Amino Acids blended with Biotin, Methionine, Lysine, Zinc and many other nutrients which have been shown to be beneficial for horses with poor hooves, such as; dry, brittle walls resulting in cracks. Enhanced growth rates and hoof hardness can be achieved with daily use of Hoof Medic. Hoof Medic’s unique blend of nutrients includes protein, vitamins, enzymes, fatty acids and trace minerals which are combined to maximize absorption while promoting thicker hoof walls, accelerated hoof growth and creating a more malleable hoof.

Recommended Dosage

Horses: Feed 1 ounce (approx 2 tablespoons) daily mixed with normal feed ration. The large end of the enclosed scoop measures 1 tablespoon. Or, as directed by a veterinarian.

Store in a cool, dry place. Reseal after each use.

For Animal Use Only.

Keep Out Of Reach Of Children.
