Computer problem 2002 Mazda tribute

The needle on the odometer, fuel gage, temperature and speedometer went all the way to the end and then all the way to the top, all the way back down and then back to normal the way it should be. All happened in a matter of seconds. Has happened twice now and I'm getting worried, does anyone know what it could be?
In Maintenance & Repair > Electrical > Gauges

    Edit Answer (for another -38 minute)

    you might check for a loose or corroded fuse # 11 in the central junction box; also check for damage underneath the dash (burned or loose wiring, or unplugged connectors).


    You can also scan the car's computer and it may show a trouble code related to the instrument cluster.
    \
    I don't think this is a GEM (General Electrical Module) issue because that module doesn't control those particular items you mentioned.
