Early Glance: Computer companies

NEW YORK — Shares of some top computer companies are up at 10 a.m.:

Apple Inc. rose $2.26 or .4 percent, to $586.88.

Dell Inc. rose $.11 or 1.2 percent, to $9.46.

Hewlett Packard Co. rose $.18 or 1.3 percent, to $14.19.

International Business Machines Corp. rose $1.79 or .9 percent, to $195.93.

Lexmark International Inc. rose $.22 or 1.0 percent, to $21.69.
