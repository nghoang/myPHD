About CNET Forums

The leading computer and technology community discussion site

CNET Forums is the best source of computer and technology troubleshooting advice from a community of experts. Forums.CNET.com features a wide range of topics, from help with Windows 7, Windows Vista, and Mac OS X to troubleshooting of consumer electronics, such as your digital camera or new HDTV.

Each forum is organized around a category of interest: iPads or iPhones, for example. Users can discuss problems and troubleshooting techniques, tips and tricks, as well as the latest news. Within each forum are threads that house the discussion about a particular problem, or technical question. Our most popular discussions happen in the forums for Windows 7, spyware, virus, & security, PC hardware, and computer help.

Our community forums include a group of truly dedicated expert moderators and also many CNET members who volunteer their personal time to share their considerable knowledge and experience with our community. So when you do get a chance to interact with them, please be sure to thank them for their time and effort.
