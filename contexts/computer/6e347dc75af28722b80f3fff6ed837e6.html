Revenue falls 3% at Computer Sciences Corp.

Washington Business Journal

Date: Tuesday, November 6, 2012, 1:15pm EST
Enlarge Image
Courtesy

Falls Church-based Computer Sciences Corp. saw revenue decline on expired Department of Defense contracts.

Falls Church-based Computer Sciences Corp. reported a revenue decline on expired Defense Department contracts, but posted a profit compared with a loss a year ago when the company took a large impairment charge.

Fiscal 2013 second-quarter revenue was $3.85 billion, compared with $3.97 billion in the same quarter a year ago. Net income was $138 million, or 83 cents per share, compared with a net loss of $2.9 billion, or $18.56 per share.
