South Side:
NOTE: The South Side ComputerWorks has moved to Lawrenceville.

This distinctive store offers a wide variety of products, including low-cost donated desktop computers, laptops, power cords, cables, monitors, hard drives, computer memory, DVD-RW burners, PC cards, expansion cards, and hard to find older computer parts, as well as new “in the box” items that are ready to go with installed hardware. We carry Apple MAC G5 computers, LCD flat screens, laptops, CPUs Intel/AMD, video cards, older software and games.

A Dell/HP desktop system, completely refurbished, for as low as $149!  It includes a computer, keyboard, mouse, 90-day warranty, Windows XP Professional and Office Suite (word processing, spreadsheets, and presentations) and is internet ready.*Windows 7 and Vista systems are available at competitive prices.

We service, upgrade and sell refurbished computers and computer accessories. We offer free, five minute system evaluations and diagnostics.  Computer repair services begins at $19.99.

The ComputerWorks store is affiliated with the Goodwill Computer Recycling Center, which repairs and recycles donated computers and related equipment. Computers and related items can be donated at any Goodwill store or Attended Donation Center.

*Subject to availability. No rainchecks. Prices subject to change without notice. Five minute system evaluation does not guarantee problem resolution. See store for details. HP/Dell/Intel/AMD are registered trademarks of the respective companies.
