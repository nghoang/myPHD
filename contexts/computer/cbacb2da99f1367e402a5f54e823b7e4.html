Certified Software Development Associate

The CSDA credential is intended for graduating software engineers and entry-level software professionals and serves to bridge the gap between your educational experience and real-world work requirements.  Register Now to get started today!  Questions? feel free to email us: csda@computer.org.

 

The CSDA is the first step towards becoming a Certified Software Development Professional (CSDP).

CSDA Eligibility

While there are no formal requirements for the CSDA, the following are recommended guidelines for candidates:

  • Recent software or computer engineering graduates
  • Under-graduates in their final year of a bachelor's degree program in software or computer engineering
  • Non-degree professionals with more than 2 years of programming experience

Fees and Testing Information

The CSDA Preparation Course includes six (6) months of access for a candidate. The CSDA examination fee includes a $50 processing fee, which is nonrefundable and nontransferable. We accept payment by Visa, MasterCard, American Express, check and wire transfer.  Checks should be made payable to the Computer Society and drawn on a US Bank.  All fees must be paid in US dollars.  For wire transfer information, please refer to the Certification FAQ.

Benefits of the CSDA


Graduates

  • Bridges the gap between your educational experience and real-world work requirements
  • Verifies your understanding of fundamental software engineering principles

Professionals

  • Shortens your training cycle and quickly increases your contribution
  • Demonstrates your commitment and level of professionalism
     

Employers

  • Reduce the new-hire training cycle
  • Protects your investment in a competent and proficient workforce
