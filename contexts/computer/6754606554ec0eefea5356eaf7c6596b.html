Owning a computer should be a fun experience. You should be able to make your computer your own without the worry. Follow these simple rules and the rest is easy!

 Install a Firewall plus Spyware and Virus software.
 Keep all software updated and this includes Windows Updates.
 Configure your computer and only install programs you use.
 Backup your computer and don't rely on System Restore.
 Disconnect from the internet when not in use.
 Remember your computer needs maintenance.
 Don't use an Administrator account online.
