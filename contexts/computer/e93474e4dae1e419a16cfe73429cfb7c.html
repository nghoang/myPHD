The Division of Business, Applied Technology & Public Service at Missouri State University-West Plains anticipates an August 2013 opening for a tenure-track Instructor of Computer Graphics & Programming. Responsibilities include teaching day, evening, or distance (including online and ITV) Game Development and/or Web Development courses on or off campus as scheduled, advising students, and other duties as assigned. Computer Information/Science related Bachelor's degree required. Salary: $32,000-$40,000/Annually. Full consideration given to applications received by 11/30/12, but applications will be accepted until position is filled.

Apply online: https://jobs.missouristate.edu. Successful candidates must be committed to working with diverse student & community populations. Employment will require a criminal background check at University expense. EO/AA
