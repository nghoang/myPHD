When I finished talking with him I still did not have a connection but was able to reset the router modem password after we hung up and had internet access then. It sounds like the LCD pixels didn't switch back. This can happen if the screen has been cold recently. All you need to do is to loosen them up. The LCD pixels are like little blinders, the more open the are the brighter the screen.

All you need to do is to close the screen and shake the laptop a little bit, that should reset the pixels.
