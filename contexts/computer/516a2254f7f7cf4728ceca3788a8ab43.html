While there certainly are a bunch of great hackers on this site, "most" of the "top" ones certainly aren't here (regardless of the metric you use to determine "top"). Linus Torvalds and Jeff Dean aren't active here, for example. Nor are Steve Wozniak and Bruce Schneier. These guys don't even have accounts, and possibly never will.

Of the folks active on Quora who have outstanding hacker credentials, I'm going to toss out my opinion and say that Anders Kaseorg and Evan Priestley are probably the best. But there are many more outstanding hackers who are *not* active on Quora. And as good as Anders and Evan are, I doubt they would put themselves in the same league as Dean or Torvalds.
