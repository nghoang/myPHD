Senior citizens with Macs invited to computer workshop

By Staff Report

November 6, 2012, 11:55 a.m.

Updated: November 7, 2012, 12:00 a.m.

  • Email
  • Post a comment
  • Print

Senior citizens interested in expanding their Macintosh computer skills are invited to attend a workshop from 9 a.m. to noon Tuesday, Nov. 27, at the Shawnee Civic Centre, 13817 Johnson Drive.

The Beginners Workshop for Mac Users is being offered by the Shawnee Parks and Recreation Department. It will teach participants the basics of using their Macintosh laptops, which they will need to bring to the class.

The fee for the class is $30, and registration is required by Nov. 20. For more information or to register, call 913-631-5200 or visitshawneeconnect.org.
