Stolen Computer Emails Home

November 6th, 2012 12:25pm

Chatham-Kent police have charged a Winter Line resident with possession of a stolen laptop after the computer told its owner where it was.

A King Street West resident reported a stolen laptop on Sunday.

The device has tracking software and not only indicated where it was located but used its built in webcam to take a picture of a man using it and then emailed that picture to the real owner’s iPhone.

Police were able to identify the man, they arrested him last night and he’s been released with a future court date.
