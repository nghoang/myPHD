Welcome to the Computer & IT telecommuting, part-time, and flexible jobs page! There are many exciting jobs to choose from in Computer and Information Technology today. These jobs can range from computer repair to web site development to Internet security positions. Computer and IT jobs involve building or operating computer and peripheral equipment to process business, scientific, engineering or other important data. Jobs in Computer and IT are continually evolving so it's important to have the proper training and understanding of how computer hardware and software works. Computer and IT careers may also be called Information Management, Technology Consulting, Web Site or Software Design and Development, Computer Solutions Engineer, Enterprise Executive, Programmer, Technical Support, Systems Analyst, and Program Manager. Jobs in Computers and IT can be contract, freelance, part time and full time. & starting salaries are typically higher than average for telecommuting jobs.
