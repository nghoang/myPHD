The College of Computer Engineering & Science at Prince Muhammad bin Fahd University - KSA is seeking to recruit faculty members with Computer Science, Computer Engineering or IT qualifications.

These positions are available from for the second (Spring) semester of the academic year 2012/2013 commencing January 2013.

Prospective candidates need to meet the following requirements:

Hold a MSc or higher in the specified disciplines from an internationally recognized university Be familiar with Programming, Databases and Web systems

Excellent spoken and written Arabic and English skills

Preference is given to candidates with some practical and/or teaching experience

Initial contract duration is for one year with possibility of renewal.

Monthly salary (tax-free) will be in accordance with academic rank, qualifications and experience. Successful applicants also receive:

60 days annual paid vacation with a maximum of 4 air tickets for self and family members Suitable housing or housing allowance Basic medical care for self and dependent family

End of service reward (on completion of at least 2 years uninterrupted service)

Applicants are required to send a letter of intent with their latest CV and contact details of two referees to:
