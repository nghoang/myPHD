Stylish and practical computer cases

A new PC case is essential, if you're building a new computer, or considering carrying out a case mod on an existing device. When choosing tower cases, things you will have to take in consideration will be its size and how will look....more

The PC case you select should suit your sense of style and options range from simple and discreet to ultra-modern and powerful looking.

As well as appearance you should be sure to check out the more technical aspects, to ensure that the case you get will allow you to create your ideal desktop pc.

For example, find out how many expansion slots, USB slots and drive bays it has, as well as the number of graphics cards that can be accommodated.

In addition, some may come with components already installed, such as cooling fans, so it is important to find out what these are and their specifications.
