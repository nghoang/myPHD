    Biography

    Brooklyn NY, United States (2010 – present)

    Computer Magic is based in Brooklyn, NY and was formed in 2010.

    The band consists of Danielle ‘Danz’ Johnson (composer, vox, synths). For live shows: James Morley plays guitar, Justin Coles plays bass, and Chris Egan plays drums.

    In March 2011, Computer Magic released a limited edition cassette entitled ‘Get a Job’ with Kill/Hurt, and a 4 track EP of re-recorded songs with White Iris entitled ‘Electronic Fences’.

    In July 2011, Computer Magic released a 7” with Roundtable Records in the UK.

    http://thecomputermagic.com/
